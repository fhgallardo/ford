SECTION 307-05: Automatic Transaxle/Transmission External Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Transmission Control Switch — Floor Shift

Removal

  1. Lift the center console lid cover and remove the console finish panel.
  1. Using a suitable tool, pry forward on the top of the selector lever button and remove the button.
  1. Remove the screw, slide the selector lever knob up and remove the selector lever knob.
  1. Remove the selector lever knob shroud.
  1. Disconnect the transmission control (TC) switch electrical connector, release the shifter bezel locking tabs and remove the selector lever bezel from the selector lever.
  1. Release the 2 tabs and push the TC switch through the selector lever bezel.

Installation

  1. Install the TC switch in the selector lever bezel.
  1. Install the selector lever bezel on the locking tabs and connect the TC switch electrical connector.
  1. Install the selector lever knob shroud.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not overtighten the screw or damage to the selector lever knob can occur.

    Install the selector lever knob and install the screw.
  1. Position the selector lever button in place and press in on the top of the button to install.
  1. Install the center console finish panel.
