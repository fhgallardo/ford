REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Remote Keyless Entry Keypad


Item Part Number Description
1 14A626 Remote keyless entry (RKE) keypad

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the exterior front door handle. For additional information, refer to Exterior Front Door Handle in this section.
  1. Release the locking tabs and remove the RKE keypad.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
