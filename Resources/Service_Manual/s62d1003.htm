SECTION 413-01: Instrument Cluster
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Instrument Cluster


Item Part Number Description
1 W707628 Instrument cluster screws (4 required)
2 130100 Transmission range indicator (if equipped)
3 — Instrument cluster electrical connectors (part of 14401) (2 required)
4 10849 Instrument cluster

Removal and Installation

  1. NOTE: Prior to removal of the instrument cluster, it is necessary to upload the module configuration information to the diagnostic tool. For additional information, refer to Section 418-01 .

    Depower the supplemental restraint system (SRS). For additional information, refer to Section 501-20B .
  1. Remove the instrument cluster center finish panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-12 .
  1. Lower the tilt steering column to the lowest position.
  1. If equipped, disconnect the transmission range indicator.
    • Push in the clips and disconnect the transmission range indicator.
  1. Remove the 4 screws and the instrument cluster.
    • Disconnect the 2 electrical connectors.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Download the instrument cluster configuration information from the diagnostic tool into the new instrument cluster. For additional information, refer to Section 418-01 .
