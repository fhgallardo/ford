Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the generator. For additional information, refer to Section 414-02 .
  1. Disconnect the cylinder head temperature (CHT) sensor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the CHT sensor.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Apply anti-seize to the threads of the CHT sensor.
