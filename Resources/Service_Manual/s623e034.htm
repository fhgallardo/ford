SECTION 303-14: Electronic Engine Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 02/22/2006

Throttle Position (TP) Sensor — 5.4L (3V)


Item Part Number Description
1 14A464 Throttle position (TP) sensor electrical connector
2 W707535 TP sensor screws (2 required)
3 9E928 TP sensor

Removal

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Disconnect the throttle position (TP) sensor electrical connector.

  1.  CAUTION: Failure to remove the TP sensor screws in the following manner will result in damage to the screws. First loosen the screws 1-2 full turns using a hand tool and then use a suitable high speed driver to complete the removal.

    Remove and discard the 2 screws and the TP sensor.

Installation

  1.  CAUTION: Do not reuse the TP sensor and screws. A new TP sensor and screws must be installed.

     CAUTION: Do not use a high speed driver to install the new screws or damage to the TP sensor can occur.

    NOTE: When installing the new TP sensor, make sure that the radial locator tab on the TP sensor is aligned with the radial locator hole on the throttle body (TB).

    Position the new TP sensor and install the 2 new screws.
    • Tighten to 3 Nm (27 lb-in).
  1. Connect the TP sensor electrical connector.
  1. Connect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
