SECTION 303-08: Engine Emission Control
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) Valve — 4.6L (2V)


Item Part Number Description
1 6K817 Positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) hose quick release coupling
2 6A666 PCV valve
3 — PCV valve O-ring seal (part of 6A666)

Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) tube quick connect coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Rotate the PCV valve counterclockwise and remove the PCV valve.
    • Discard the PCV valve.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Install a new PCV valve.
