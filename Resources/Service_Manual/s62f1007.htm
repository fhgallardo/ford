SECTION 415-01: Audio Unit
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Satellite Radio Receiver


Item Part Number Description
1 18806 Satellite radio receiver
2 W707628 Satellite radio receiver screws (4 required)
3 — Satellite radio receiver electrical/antenna connectors (part of 14401)

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the audio unit. For additional information, refer to Audio Unit in this section.
  1. Remove the 4 screws and the satellite radio receiver.
    • Disconnect the electrical and antenna connectors.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
