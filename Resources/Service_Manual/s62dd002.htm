2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Parking Aid

The parking aid system consists of the following:

  • Four ultrasonic parking aid sensors located in the rear bumper
  • Parking aid module located on the cowl above and next to the brake pedal
  • Parking aid speaker — audible warning tone, located on the parking aid module
  • Parking aid switch located on the instrument panel below the climate control assembly
