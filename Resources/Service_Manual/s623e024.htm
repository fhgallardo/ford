SECTION 303-14: Electronic Engine Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 02/02/2005

Intake Manifold Runner Control (IMRC) Actuator — 4.2L


Item Part Number Description
1 14489 Intake manifold runner control (IMRC) actuator electrical connector
2 0069 LH IMRC actuator rod
3 0068 RH IMRC actuator rod
4 N806549 IMRC actuator bolts (3 required)
5 9J559 IMRC actuator

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the upper intake manifold. For additional information, refer to Section 303-01A .
  1. Disconnect the intake manifold runner control (IMRC) electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the IMRC actuator rods.
  1. Remove the bolts and the IMRC actuator.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
