SECTION 413-01: Instrument Cluster
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Instrument Cluster Lens

  1. Remove the instrument cluster. For additional information, refer to Instrument Cluster in this section.
  1. Release the tabs and remove the instrument cluster lens.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
