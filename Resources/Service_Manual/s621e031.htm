2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Door Lock Control Switch

NOTE: LH side shown, RH side similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 SP7814 Switch plate cover
2 — Switch plate electrical connectors (part of 14A488)
3 14028 Door lock control switch

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the switch plate cover.
  1. Disconnect the switch plate electrical connectors.
  1. Release the door lock control switch tabs on the switch plate and remove the door lock control switch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
