SECTION 303-04B: Fuel Charging and Controls — 4.6L (2V)
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Fuel Pump Driver Module (FPDM)


Item Part Number Description
1 14A464 Fuel pump driver module (FPDM) electrical connector (part of 14405)
2 W706644-S FPDM bolt (2 required)
3 9D373 FPDM
  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .

  1. NOTE: The fuel pump driver module (FPDM) is located above the spare tire.

    Disconnect the FPDM electrical connector.
  1. Remove the FPDM bolts and the FPDM.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
