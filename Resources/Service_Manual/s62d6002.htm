SECTION 413-06: Horn
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Horn

The horn system consists of the following components:

  • horn switch (part of the driver air bag module)
  • clockspring
  • horn relay (part of the central junction box [CJB])
  • horn

For removal and installation of the driver air bag module, refer to Section 501-20B .
