SECTION 413-13: Parking Aid
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/09/2005

Parking Aid Switch


Item Part Number Description
1 15A860 Parking aid switch

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the instrument panel center finish panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-12 .
  1. Press the retaining tabs to remove the parking aid switch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
