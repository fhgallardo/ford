SECTION 303-12: Intake Air Distribution and Filtering
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 02/02/2005

Air Cleaner Intake Pipe — 5.4L (3V)

Removal and Installation

  1. Release the clamps and remove the air cleaner tray.
  1. Remove the air cleaner intake pipe bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Reach into the opening of the air cleaner assembly and depress the locking tabs on the air cleaner intake pipe and resonator to release the air cleaner intake pipe and resonator from the air cleaner assembly. Remove the air cleaner intake pipe.

  1.  CAUTION: The air cleaner intake tube inlet rubber grommet must be seated flush against the inner fender prior to air cleaner intake tube installation.

    Remove the air cleaner intake tube inlet rubber grommet from the inner fender.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
