Removal and Installation

NOTE: Installation of a new suction accumulator is not required when repairing the air conditioning system, except when there is physical evidence of contamination from a failed A/C compressor or damage to the suction accumulator.

  1. Disconnect the battery. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Recover the refrigerant. For additional information, refer to Section 412-00 .
  1. Remove the powertrain control module (PCM). For additional information, refer to Section 303-14 .
  1. Disconnect the A/C cycling switch electrical connector.
  1. Remove the PCM bracket.
    1. Remove the 3 PCM bracket bolts.
    1. Remove the PCM bracket.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Disconnect the evaporator inlet fitting.
    • Discard the O-ring seals.
  1. Disconnect the condenser outlet fitting.
    • Discard the O-ring seal.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the condenser to evaporator line.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Install new O-ring seals.
    • Lubricate the refrigerant system with the correct amount of clean PAG oil. For additional information, refer to Section 412-00 .
  1. Evacuate, leak test and charge the refrigerant system. For additional information, refer to Section 412-00 .
