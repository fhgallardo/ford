SECTION 303-14: Electronic Engine Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 02/02/2005

Charge Motion Control Valve (CMCV) — 5.4L (3V)

NOTE: The charge motion control valve (CMCV) is sometimes referred to as the intake manifold runner control (IMRC) actuator in the Powertrain Control/Emissions Diagnosis (PC/ED) manual.


Item Part Number Description
1 14A464 Charge motion control valve (CMCV) electrical connector
2 9B841 RH CMCV rod
3 9B842 LH CMCV rod
4 W709084 CMCV stud bolt
5 W708165 CMCV bolts (2 required)
6 9L490 CMCV

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the intake manifold. For additional information, refer to Section 303-01C .
  1. Disconnect the charge motion control valve (CMCV) rods.
  1. Remove the stud bolt, the bolts and the CMCV.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
