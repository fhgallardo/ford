SECTION 501-11: Glass, Frames and Mechanisms
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Window Regulator and Motor — Front Door

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the front door trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Remove the front door watershield.
  1. Secure the front door window glass in the full UPWARD position with tape.
  1. Remove the front door window glass-to-regulator assembly bolts.
    • Loosen the glass clamp fasteners through the 2 vertical slots in the inner door panel.
    • To install, tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Disconnect the front door window electrical connector.
  1. Remove the front door window regulator bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 11 Nm (8 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the front door window regulator and motor.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
