SECTION 303-08: Engine Emission Control
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) Valve — 4.2L


Item Part Number Description
1 14A464 Positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve electrical connector
2 6758 PCV valve hose
3 6769 PCV valve

Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Disconnect the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the quick connect couplings and remove the PCV tube. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .

  1. NOTE: Anytime the PCV valve is removed, a new PCV valve must be installed.

    Rotate the PCV valve counterclockwise and remove the PCV valve.
    • Discard the PCV valve.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
