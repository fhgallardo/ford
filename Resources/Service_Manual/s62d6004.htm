SECTION 413-06: Horn
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Horn


Item Part Number Description
1 W709435 Horn bolt
2 — Horn electrical connector (part of 14398)
3 13832 Horn

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the 5 push pin retainers holding the RH radiator side air deflector to the core support.
  1. Remove the bolt and the horn assembly.
    • Disconnect the electrical connector.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
