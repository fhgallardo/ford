SECTION 303-01C: Engine — 5.4L (3V)
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
IN-VEHICLE REPAIR
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Camshaft Roller Follower


Special Tool(s)
Compressor, Valve Spring
303-1039

Material
Item Specification
Motorcraft SAE 5W-20 Premium Synthetic Blend Motor Oil
XO-5W20-QSP (in Canada Motorcraft SAE 5W-20 Super Premium Motor Oil CXO-5W20-LSP12) or equivalent 		WSS-M2C930-A

Removal

  1. Depending on the camshaft roller follower being serviced, remove the LH or RH valve cover. For additional information, refer to Valve Cover — LH or Valve Cover — RH in this section.
  1. Rotate the crankshaft until the piston for the valve being serviced is at the top of its stroke with the intake valve and the exhaust valves closed.

  1.  CAUTION: If the components are to be reinstalled, they must be installed in the same positions. Mark the components for installation into their original locations.

    Using the special tool, compress the valve spring and remove the camshaft roller follower.
  1. Repeat the previous 2 steps for each camshaft roller follower being serviced.
  1. Inspect the camshaft roller follower. For additional information, refer to Section 303-00 .

Installation

  1.  CAUTION: If the components are to be reinstalled, they must be installed into their original locations.

    NOTE: Lubricate the camshaft roller follower with clean engine oil prior to installation.

    Using the special tool, compress the valve spring and install the camshaft roller follower.

  1. NOTE: Lubricate the camshaft roller follower with clean engine oil prior to installation.

    Repeat the previous step for each camshaft roller follower being serviced.
  1. Depending on the valve being serviced, install the LH or RH valve cover. For additional information, refer to Valve Cover — LH or Valve Cover — RH in this section.
