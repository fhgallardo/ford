SECTION 501-09: Rear View Mirrors
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 01/10/2005

Exterior Mirror Motor

Removal and Installation

NOTE: If equipped with power folding mirrors, they must be synchronized any time the mirrors have been folded or unfolded without using the mirror control switch. Refer to Exterior Mirror Motor Synchronization — Power Folding in this section.

  1. Remove the exterior rear view mirror glass. For additional information, refer to Exterior Mirror Glass in this section.
  1. Remove the 3 exterior rear view mirror motor screws.
  1. Disconnect the exterior rear view mirror motor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the exterior rear view mirror motor.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • If equipped, synchronize the power folding mirrors. Refer to Exterior Mirror Motor Synchronization — Power Folding in this section.
