SECTION 303-14: Electronic Engine Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 02/02/2005

Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor — 4.6L (2V)


Item Part Number Description
1 9628 Air cleaner clamp
2 14A464 Mass air flow (MAF) sensor electrical connector
3 — Grommet (part of 12A690)
4 9A628 Air cleaner inner cover
5 12A690 MAF sensor electrical connector
6 W705761 MAF sensor bolt (2 required)
7 12B579 MAF sensor

Removal and Installation

  1. Open the clamp on the air cleaner and separate the tray from the cover.
  1. Disconnect the mass air flow (MAF) sensor electrical connector and detach the connector retainer.
  1. Remove the grommet from the air cleaner cover and slide it down the electrical harness.
  1. Pull the air cleaner inner cover and MAF sensor out of the air cleaner cover.
  1. Disconnect the MAF sensor electrical connector and remove the inner cover and MAF sensor assembly.
  1. Remove the bolts and the MAF sensor.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
