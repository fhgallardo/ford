Paint Codes

Vehicle Certification Label Locator

Vehicle exterior paint codes can be found on the vehicle certification label. All colors are base coat/clear coat.

Paint codes are listed as a 2-part code. The first set of characters listed identifies the vehicle primary paint color. The second set of characters listed (if applicable) identifies the vehicle accent or 2-tone color.

    Primary Color Codes
  • AQ — Arizona Beige — F-150/Mark LT
  • CX — Dark Shadow Grey — F-150/Mark LT
  • E4 — Vermilion — F-150
  • F6 — Ceramic White — F-150
  • HG — Smokestone — F-150/Mark LT
  • JL — Dark Toreador Red — F-150/Mark LT
  • L2 — True Blue Pearl — F-150
  • LD — Medium Wedgewood Blue — F-150
  • P5 — Aspen Green Metallic — F-150
  • T5 — Dark Copper — F-150
  • T7 — Charcoal Beige — F-150
  • UA — Ebony — F-150/Mark LT
  • YN — Silver metallic — F-150/Mark LT
  • YZ — Oxford White — F-150/Mark LT
    Accent or 2-Tone Color Codes
  • AQ — Arizona Beige — F-150
  • CX — Dark Shadow Gray — F-150
  • YN — Silver Metallic — F-150
