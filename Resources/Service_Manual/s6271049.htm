SECTION 307-01: Automatic Transmission — 4R70E/4R75E
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
IN-VEHICLE REPAIR
Procedure revision date: 03/14/2006

Transmission Insulator and Retainer

Removal

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. If equipped, remove the transfer case skid plate.
  1. Remove the 2 bolts for the exhaust hanger.
  1. Remove the rear transmission insulator nuts.
  1. Install a suitable transmission jack.
  1. Slightly raise the rear of the transmission and remove the bolts. Remove the rear transmission insulator.

Installation

  1. Install the transmission insulator and the bolts.
    • Tighten to 80 Nm (59 lb-ft).
  1. Lower the transmission onto the crossmember and install the nuts.
    • Tighten to 103 Nm (76 lb-ft).
  1. Install the bolts retaining the exhaust hanger to the crossmember.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. If equipped, install the transfer case skid plate.
    • Tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
