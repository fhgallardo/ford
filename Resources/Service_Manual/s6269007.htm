SECTION 206-09: Vehicle Dynamic Systems
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Wheel Speed Sensor — Front, Four Wheel Drive (4WD)


Item Part Number Description
1 14A624 Wheel speed sensor electrical connector
2 — Wheel speed sensor harness retainers (4 required)
3 2027 Brake caliper anchor plate bolts (2 required)
4 — Brake caliper assembly
5 2C498 Brake disc
6 2B663 Wheel speed sensor bolt
7 2C204 Wheel speed sensor

Removal and Installation

 CAUTION: When removing the front disc brake components, never allow them to hang from the brake hose.

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .

  1. NOTE: The harness connector is located in the engine compartment secured to the fender apron.

    Disconnect the wheel speed sensor electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the 4 wheel speed sensor harness retainers.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not allow the brake caliper to hang from the brake hose or damage to the hose can occur.

    Remove the 2 brake caliper anchor plate bolts and position the brake caliper, pads and anchor plate aside.
    • Support the brake caliper, pads and anchor plate using mechanic's wire.
    • To install, tighten to 112 Nm (83 lb-ft).

  1. NOTE: Index-mark the brake disc and wheel hub flange before removing the brake disc.

    Remove the brake disc.
  1. Remove the bolt and the wheel speed sensor.
    • To install, tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
