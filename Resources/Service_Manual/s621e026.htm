Tailgate Release Handle


Material
Item Specification
Multi-Purpose Grease Spray XL-5
ESB-M1C93-B

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the tailgate remote control. For additional information, refer to Tailgate Latch Remote Control in this section.
  1. Remove the tailgate release handle.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Lubricate the tailgate release handle after installation.
