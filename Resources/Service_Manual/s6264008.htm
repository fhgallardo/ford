Removal

  1. Remove the rear axle. For additional information, refer to Section 205-02A , Section 205-02B or Section 205-02C .
  1. Remove the parking brake shoes. For additional information, refer to Section 206-05 .
  1. Remove the 4 brake caliper support bracket nuts and bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 81 Nm (60 lb-ft).
  1. Separate the brake disc shield from the brake caliper support bracket and the rear axle housing.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
