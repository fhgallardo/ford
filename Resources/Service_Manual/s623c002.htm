Intake Air Distribution and Filtering

The intake air system consists of the:

  • engine air cleaner (ACL).
  • air cleaner (ACL) element.
  • air cleaner clamp.
  • mass air flow (MAF) sensor plate.
  • MAF sensor.
  • air cleaner outlet pipe (4.2L and 4.6L (2V)).
  • air cleaner intake pipe (5.4L (3V)).

The intake air system:

  • filters take in air with a replaceable, dry-type air cleaner element.
  • measures air flow and air temperature with a MAF sensor.
