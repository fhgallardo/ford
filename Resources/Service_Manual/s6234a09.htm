SECTION 303-04A: Fuel Charging and Controls — 4.2L
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Fuel Pump Driver Module (FPDM)


Item Part Number Description
1 14A464 Fuel pump driver module (FPDM) electrical connector
2 W706644 FPDM bolts (2 required)
3 9D370 FPDM

Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Lower the spare tire.
  1. Disconnect the fuel pump driver module electrical connector.
  1. Remove the bolts and the fuel pump driver module (FPDM).
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
