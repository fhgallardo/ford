Removal

  1. Remove the A/C compressor. For additional information, refer to Air Conditioning (A/C) Compressor — 4.2L or Air Conditioning (A/C) Compressor — 4.6L, 5.4L in this section.
  1. Remove the A/C clutch disc and hub bolt.
    1. Using the special tool, hold the A/C clutch disc and hub.
    1. Remove the A/C clutch disc and hub bolt.
  1. Remove the A/C clutch disc and hub.
  1. Remove the A/C clutch disc and hub spacer(s).
  1. Remove the A/C clutch pulley snap ring.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not use air tools. Damage to the A/C compressor pulley or A/C compressor may result.

    Remove the A/C compressor pulley.
    1. Install the special tool.
    1. Remove the A/C compressor pulley.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not use air tools. Damage to the A/C clutch field coil or A/C compressor may result.

    Remove the A/C clutch field coil.
    1. Install the special tool.
    1. Install the special tool.
    1. Remove the A/C clutch field coil.

Installation

NOTE: If installing a new A/C compressor, the A/C clutch components should be reused unless obvious signs of damage are found. If excessive grooving is found, a new A/C clutch disc and hub and A/C compressor pulley must be installed together. Otherwise, each component can be installed individually where needed.

  1. Visually inspect the A/C clutch disc and hub, A/C compressor pulley and A/C clutch field coil for damage.
    • Inspect for physical damage, including cracked or melted components or discoloration due to excessive heat.
    • Inspect for excessive wear, including grooving in the A/C clutch disc and hub or A/C compressor pulley that is more than fingernail depth.
    • Inspect for roughness in the A/C compressor pulley bearing.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not use air tools. Damage to the A/C clutch field coil or A/C compressor may result.

    Install the A/C clutch field coil.
    1. Place the A/C clutch field coil on the A/C compressor with the A/C clutch field coil electrical connector correctly positioned.
    1. Place the special tool on the A/C clutch field coil.
    1. Place special tool 412-065 on special tool 412-078.
    1. Use the special tool to install the A/C clutch field coil.
  1. Install the A/C compressor pulley.
  1. Install the A/C compressor pulley snap ring.
  1. Install the A/C clutch disc and hub spacer(s) and the A/C clutch disc and hub.
  1. Install the A/C clutch disc and hub bolt.
    1. Hold the A/C clutch disc and hub with the special tool
    1. Tighten to 14 Nm (10 lb-ft).
  1. Measure and adjust the clutch air gap by adding or removing A/C clutch disc and hub spacers.
  1. Install the A/C compressor. For additional information, refer to Air Conditioning (A/C) Compressor — 4.2L or Air Conditioning (A/C) Compressor — 4.6L, 5.4L in this section.
