SECTION 308-07B: Transfer Case
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL
Procedure revision date: 07/29/2005

Transfer Case — Torque On Demand (TOD)

  1. NOTE: Index-mark the driveshaft to maintain initial driveshaft balance during installation.

    Remove the front driveshaft. For additional information, refer to Section 205-01 .

  1. NOTE: To maintain initial driveshaft balance, index-mark the rear driveshaft.

    Remove the rear driveshaft. For additional information, refer to Section 205-01 .
  1. Remove the skid plate, if equipped.
    • To install, tighten to 18 Nm (13 lb-ft).
  1. Drain the fluid if the transfer case is to be disassembled. For additional information, refer to Transfer Case Draining and Filling in this section.
  1. Disconnect the transfer case electrical connector (located on the extension housing).

  1.  CAUTION: Secure the transfer case to the jack with safety strap.

    Using a suitable jack, support the transfer case. Securely strap the transfer case to the jack.
  1. Remove the transfer case.
    • Remove the 6 transfer case-to-transmission bolts and separate the transfer case from the transmission.
    • To install, tighten to 47 Nm (35 lb-ft).
      • Tighten the transmission-to-transfer case bolts evenly in a star pattern.
    • Separate the transfer case from the transmission.
  1. Lower the transfer case from the vehicle.

  1.  CAUTION: Carefully clean the gasket surfaces. Nicks and gouges cause fluid leaks.

    Remove the transfer case-to-transmission gasket and clean the mating surfaces.
