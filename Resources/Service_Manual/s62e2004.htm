Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the battery. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Rotate the front end accessory drive belt tensioner counterclockwise and position the front end accessory drive belt aside.
  1. Disconnect the generator electrical connector.
  1. Position the generator B+ protective boot aside and remove the nut and disconnect the generator B+ terminal.
    • To install, tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
  1. Remove the nut and position the A/C line bracket aside.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the nut and position the generator harness bracket aside.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 2 generator stud bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the bolt and the generator.
    • To install, tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
