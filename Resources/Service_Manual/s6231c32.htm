Removal

All vehicles

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Remove the throttle body (TB). For additional information, refer to Section 303-04C .
  1. Remove the 3 bolts and position the power steering fluid reservoir aside.
  1. Remove the generator. For additional information, refer to Section 414-02 .
  1. Remove the upper radiator sight shield.
  1. Remove the bolts and position the cooling fan shroud away from the radiator.
  1. Install the special tool.
  1. If equipped, remove the 4 bolts and the skid plate.
  1. Remove the 4 exhaust manifold-to-catalytic converter nuts.
  1. Remove the exhaust system support bracket bolts from the crossmember.
  1. Loosen the transmission mount nuts.

RH engine support insulator

  1. Remove the starter. For additional information, refer to Section 303-06 .

LH engine support insulator

  1. Remove the oil level indicator and tube. For additional information, refer to Oil Level Indicator and Tube in this section.
  1. If removing the LH engine support insulator in a 4WD vehicle, index-mark the front driveshaft flange to the front axle pinion flange.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not let the driveshaft hang unsupported.

    If removing the LH engine support insulator in a 4WD vehicle, disconnect and support the front driveshaft.
    • Remove the 4 bolts.
    • Support the front driveshaft.

All vehicles

  1. Remove the RH engine support insulator nuts and washer.
  1. Remove the LH engine support insulator bolt.
  1. Using the special tools, raise the engine.

RH engine support insulator

  1. Remove the 3 RH engine support insulator bracket-to-cylinder block bolts.
  1. Remove the RH engine support insulator.

LH engine support insulator

  1. Remove the 3 LH engine support insulator bracket-to-cylinder block bolts.
  1. Remove the LH engine support insulator.

Installation

LH engine support insulator

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator-to-bracket mating surface of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Position the LH engine support insulator into the vehicle.

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator bracket-to-cylinder block mating surfaces of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Install the 3 LH engine support insulator bracket-to-cylinder block bolts.
    • Apply Threadlock 262 to the bolt threads prior to installation.
    • Tighten to 63 Nm (46 lb-ft).

RH engine support insulator

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator-to-bracket mating surface of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Position the RH engine support insulator into the vehicle.

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator bracket-to-cylinder block mating surfaces of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Install the 3 RH engine support insulator bracket-to-cylinder block bolts.
    • Apply Threadlock 262 to the bolt threads prior to installation.
    • Tighten to 63 Nm (46 lb-ft).

All vehicles

  1. Lower the engine.

  1.  CAUTION: Only use hand tools when installing the LH engine mount bolt or damage to the engine mount can occur.

    Apply Threadlock 262 to the threads and install the LH engine support insulator bolt.
    • Tighten to 350 Nm (255 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: Make sure the RH engine support insulator mating surfaces and the washer mating surface are free of foreign material and corrosion before installation. Install a new washer.

     CAUTION: Only use hand tools when installing the RH engine mount nuts or damage to the engine mount can occur.

    Apply Threadlock 262 to the stud threads and install the RH engine support insulator washer and nuts.
    • Tighten to 250 Nm (184 lb-ft).

LH engine support insulator

  1. If installing the LH engine support insulator in a 4WD vehicle, align the front driveshaft flange to the front axle pinion flange at the index mark and install the bolts.
    • Tighten to 111 Nm (82 lb-ft).
  1. Install the oil level indicator and tube. For additional information, refer to Oil Level Indicator and Tube in this section.

RH engine support insulator

  1. Install the starter. For additional information, refer to Section 303-06 .

All vehicles

  1. Tighten the transmission mount nuts.
    • Tighten to 103 Nm (76 lb-ft).
  1. Install the exhaust system support bracket bolts to the crossmember.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Install the 4 exhaust manifold-to-catalytic converter nuts.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. If equipped, position the skid plate and install the 4 bolts.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Position the cooling fan shroud and install the bolts.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Install the upper radiator sight shield.
  1. Install the generator. For additional information, refer to Section 414-02 .
  1. Position the power steering fluid reservoir and install the 3 bolts.
    • Tighten to 11 Nm (8 lb-ft).
  1. Install the throttle body. For additional information, refer to Section 303-04C .
  1. Connect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
