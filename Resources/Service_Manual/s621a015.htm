2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Seat — Front, 20 Percent


Item Part Number Description
1 62900 20 percent seat cushion
2 14A005 Electrical disconnect (if equipped)
3 W710661 20 percent riser-to-floor front bolt (2 required)
4 W710680 20 percent riser-to-floor rear bolt (2 required)
5 64924 20 percent seat backrest assembly

Removal and Installation

NOTE: Due to locator pin location on the riser frame, 20 percent seats will not interchange between regular cab vehicles and SuperCab/SuperCrew vehicles.

  1. Remove the seat cushion.
    • While applying slight pressure, pull up at the front edge of the seat cushion, lift the cushion up and pull the cushion out.
  1. Disconnect the electrical disconnect (if equipped).
  1. Remove the 20 percent riser-to-floor front bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 20 percent riser-to-floor rear bolts
    • To install, tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Make sure the safety belts and buckles are accessible to the occupants after installation.
