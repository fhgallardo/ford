Exterior Mirror Motor Synchronization — Power Folding

NOTE: This procedure must be performed any time the power folding mirrors are manually folded in or out, or if the position of the mirror case has been moved without using the mirror control switch.

NOTE: If the power folding mirrors are electronically folded or unfolded continuously (6 to 10 cycles), the power lockout feature will disable the system for 2 minutes to prevent damage to the power fold motors. After 2 minutes have elapsed, normal operation will resume.

  1. Set the mirror control switch to the CENTER position.

NOTE: It may be necessary to cycle the power folding mirrors in or out up to 4 times before they are synchronized.

  1. Use the mirror control switch (press rearward) to automatically cycle the power folding mirrors in and out 4 times.
  1. When an audible popping sound is heard, the power folding mirror motors are synchronized.
