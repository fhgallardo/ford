SECTION 501-14: Handles, Locks, Latches and Entry Systems
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Exterior Door Handle — SuperCrew

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear door trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Position the watershield aside.
  1. Open the clip and disconnect the exterior rear door handle actuating rod.
  1. Remove the 2 exterior rear door handle nuts.
    • To install, tighten to 7 Nm (62 lb-in).
  1. Remove the 2 exterior rear door handle seals.
  1. Remove the 2 exterior rear door handle bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 7 Nm (62 lb-in).
  1. Remove the exterior rear door handle.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
