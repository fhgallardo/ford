Removal and Installation

 WARNING: Do not smoke or carry lighted tobacco or open flame of any type when working on or near any fuel-related components. Highly flammable mixtures are always present and may be ignited. Failure to follow these instructions may result in personal injury.

 CAUTION: The throttle body must be removed from the vehicle to be cleaned. Do not hold the throttle plate open with any object that could scratch the bore or plate while servicing or cleaning the throttle body.

  1. Remove the air cleaner outlet tube. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Disconnect the electronic throttle body electrical connector.
  1. Remove the bolts and the electronic throttle body.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Inspect the throttle body gasket. Install a new gasket, if necessary.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
