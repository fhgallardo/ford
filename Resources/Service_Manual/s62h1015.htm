SECTION 417-01: Exterior Lighting
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Headlamp Switch


Item Part Number Description
1 14401 Headlamp switch electrical connectors (2 required)
2 11654 Headlamp switch

Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the battery. For additional information, refer to Section 414-00 .
  1. Detach the headlamp switch assembly.
  1. Disconnect the headlamp switch electrical connector.
  1. Remove the headlamp switch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
