2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 08/11/2005

Trailer Hitch

Removal and Installation

NOTE: The trailer hitch is integral to the rear bumper.

NOTE: F-150 Flareside shown, all others similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 — Bumper cover (LH/RH)
2 N811645-S Upper trailer hitch assembly-to-bumper bolts (4 required)
3 W504042-S Trailer hitch electrical connector screws (4 required)
4 14A624 Trailer hitch electrical connector
5 W505435-S Lower trailer hitch assembly-to-bumper bolts (2 required)
6 17D826 Trailer hitch assembly
7 — Rear bumper assembly
  1. Remove the rear bumper. For additional information, refer to Section 501-19 .
  1. Remove the 4 upper trailer hitch assembly-to-bumper bolts.
    • Remove the rear bumper cover(s) to gain access.
    • To install, tighten to 175 Nm (129 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 2 lower trailer hitch assembly-to-bumper bolts and the trailer hitch assembly.
    • To install, tighten to 175 Nm (129 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
