SECTION 501-09: Rear View Mirrors
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Exterior Mirror

Removal and Installation

NOTE: If equipped with power folding mirrors, they must be synchronized any time the mirrors have been folded or unfolded without using the mirror control switch. Refer to Exterior Mirror Motor Synchronization — Power Folding in this section.

  1. Remove the front door trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Position aside the water shield to gain access to the electrical connector.
    • Disconnect the electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 3 nuts.
    • To install, tighten to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the exterior mirror.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • If equipped with power folding mirrors, they must be synchronized. For additional information, refer to Exterior Mirror Motor Synchronization — Power Folding in this section.
