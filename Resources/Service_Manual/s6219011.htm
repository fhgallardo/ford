SECTION 501-09: Rear View Mirrors
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 01/10/2005

Exterior Mirror Control Switch


Item Part Number Description
1 14B134 Front door switch panel
2 180103-14631 Electrical connectors
3 17B676 Exterior mirror control switch

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the front door switch panel.
    • Disconnect the electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the exterior mirror control switch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
