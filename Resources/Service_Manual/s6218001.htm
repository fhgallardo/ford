SECTION 501-08: Exterior Trim and Ornamentation
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/09/2006

Window Moulding

NOTE: LH side shown, RH side similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 1522596 RH/ 1522597 LH Outer belt moulding
2 1520554 Window applique
3 151452 RH/ 151453 LH Glass run

Removal

  1. Remove the outer belt moulding.
    • Release the retaining clip at the rear of the belt moulding, then slide the front of the moulding off of the flange to remove the belt moulding from the door.

  1. NOTE: The window applique is held on by double-sided tape from the forward edge of the moulding and by trim clips near the center of the moulding. The rearmost edge rolls over the door frame sheet metal.

    Starting at the upper forward corner of the applique, insert a suitable removal tool between the applique and the door frame.
  1. Determine the location of the double-sided tape by spreading carefully, spray on adhesive cleaner down the space between the applique and door frame. Allow the cleaner to set for several minutes.
  1. Using the removal tool, begin removing the applique, spraying additional adhesive cleaner as needed.

Installation

  1. Remove all remaining adhesive tape on the mating surfaces of the door and apply new adhesive tape.

  1. NOTE: Make sure the forward edge of the applique is underneath the glass run lip.

    Install the window applique on the edge of the door, engage the groove then gently press down to engage the clips and tape.
  1. Install the outer belt moulding.
