2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DISASSEMBLY AND ASSEMBLY OF SUBASSEMBLIES
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Reverse Sun Gear

Disassembly


Item Part Number Description
1 7C096 Forward clutch hub bearing No. 4
2 7D234 Forward clutch sun gear bearing No. 5 (2-piece bearing)
3 7A399 Forward clutch sun gear
4 7A019 Reverse sun gear assembly
  1. Remove the reverse clutch sun gear assembly and the No. 4 forward clutch hub bearing and race.
  1. Remove the forward clutch sun gear assembly and the No. 5 forward clutch sun gear bearing and race.

Assembly

  1. The forward clutch sun gear and reverse sun gear assembly are assembled as part of the transmission assembly procedure.
