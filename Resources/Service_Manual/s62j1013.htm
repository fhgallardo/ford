SECTION 419-01: Anti-Theft
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Passive Anti-Theft System (PATS) Transceiver


Item Part Number Description
1 504041 Steering column shroud bolts (2 required)
2 3K512 Steering column shroud, lower
3 — Ignition lock cylinder cover
4 14401 PATS transceiver electrical connector (part of 14401)
5 W707658 PATS transceiver screw
6 15607 PATS transceiver

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the steering wheel. For additional information, refer to Section 211-04 .
  1. Remove the 2 bolts and the lower steering column shroud.

  1. NOTE: Vehicles with a steering column shift are shown; the floor console shift is similar.

    Remove the ignition lock cylinder cover.
  1. Disconnect the PATS transceiver electrical connector.
  1. Remove the screw and the PATS transceiver.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
