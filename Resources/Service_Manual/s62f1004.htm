SECTION 415-01: Audio Unit
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 08/11/2006

Audio Unit


Item Part Number Description
1 1504302 Center instrument panel finish panel
2 W707628 Audio unit screws (4 required)
3 18C869 Audio unit
4 14401 Audio unit electrical connectors and antenna lead

Removal and Installation

NOTE: It is not necessary to remove the audio unit to retrieve the part number. For additional information, refer to Section 415-00 , Audio Unit Part Number Retrieval.

  1. Remove the center instrument panel finish panel.
    • Disconnect the electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the 4 screws and the audio unit.
    • Disconnect the electrical connectors and the antenna lead.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
