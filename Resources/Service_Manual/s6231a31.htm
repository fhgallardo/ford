Removal

  1. Remove the flywheel or the flexplate. For additional information, refer to Flywheel or Flexplate in this section.
  1. Using the special tools, remove the crankshaft rear seal.

Installation

  1. Clean and inspect the mating surface.

  1. NOTE: Lubricate the crankshaft rear seal lips with clean engine oil prior to installation.

    Assemble the special tools and the crankshaft rear seal.
  1. Install the special tools on the rear of the crankshaft.
  1. Install the crankshaft rear seal. Tighten the center jack screw until the spacer contacts the engine block.
  1. Install the flywheel or the flexplate. For additional information, refer to Flywheel or Flexplate in this section.
