SECTION 501-12: Instrument Panel and Console
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Console — Floor


Item Part Number Description
1 N811040 Push pins (3 required)
2 1504609/ 1504608 Floor trim panel (2 required) (LH/RH)
3 15011202 Shifter trim panel
4 7E395 Shifter cable
5 14401 Electrical connector
6 W704734-S424 Floor console bolts (4 required)
7 15011202 Floor console

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the 3 floor trim panel push pins.
  1. Remove the 2 floor trim panels.
  1. Remove the shifter trim panel.
  1. Disconnect the shifter cable.
  1. Disconnect the floor console electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 4 floor console bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 6 Nm (53 lb-in).
  1. Remove the floor console.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
