Disassembly and Assembly

NOTE: Due to locator pin location on the riser frame, 20 percent seat riser frames will not interchange between regular cab vehicles and SuperCab/SuperCrew vehicles.

All seats

  1. Remove the 20 percent seat. For additional information, refer to Seat — Front, 20 Percent in this section.
  1. Remove the LH side shield pin-type retainer, screw and shield.
  1. Remove the RH upper side shield screw and shield.
  1. Remove the RH lower side shield screw and shield.

Seats with storage armrest

  1. Remove the cupholder assembly screws.
    • Disconnect the electrical connector and remove the cupholder assembly.

All seats

  1. Remove the safety belt buckle or safety belt mini-buckle bolt and the buckle/mini-buckle on the LH side.
    • To install, tighten to 55 Nm (41 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the lap safety belt or safety belt buckle bolt and the safety belt/buckle on the RH side.
    • To install, tighten to 55 Nm (41 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the LH backrest bracket pivot bolt and pivot bolt bushing.
    • To install, tighten to 35 Nm (26 lb-ft).

Seats with storage armrest

  1. Remove the backrest stop bolt.

All seats

  1. Remove the 2 RH upper adjustable backrest bracket bolts and the adjustable backrest.
    • To install, tighten to 35 Nm (26 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the RH lower adjustable backrest bracket screw, 2 bolts and the adjustable backrest bracket.
  1. Remove the LH lower backrest pivot bracket screw, 2 bolts and the backrest pivot bracket.

Seats without storage armrest

  1. Unzip the armrest trim cover zipper, peal the trim cover back and remove the hog rings and the trim cover.

All seats

  1. To assemble, reverse the disassembly procedure.
