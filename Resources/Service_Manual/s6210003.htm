2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Insulation

Insulation is installed:

  • under the roof.
  • above and below the instrument panel.
  • at the cowl side panels.
  • over the front and rear tunnel.
  • over the front and rear floor pans.

It has heat-bondable mastic deadeners for improved noise, vibration and harshness (NVH) characteristics.
