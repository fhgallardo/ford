REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Tailgate Latch


Material
Item Specification
Multi-Purpose Grease Spray XL-5
ESB-M1C93-B

Removal and Installation

  1. Open and support the tailgate.
  1. Remove the 8 screws and the tailgate access panel.
    • To install, tighten to 5 Nm (44 lb-in).
  1. Open the clip and release the tailgate latch actuating rod from the tailgate remote control.
  1. Release the tailgate latch actuating rod locator from the tailgate.
  1. Remove the tailgate latch bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the tailgate latch bolt and the tailgate latch cable.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the tailgate latch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Lubricate the tailgate latch after installation.
