SECTION 303-12: Intake Air Distribution and Filtering
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Air Cleaner Outlet Pipe — 4.6L (2V)

Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the crankcase breather hose from the air cleaner outlet pipe.
  1. Loosen the clamps at the throttle body and the air cleaner cover. Remove the air cleaner outlet pipe.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
