SECTION 303-12: Intake Air Distribution and Filtering
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 09/07/2005

Air Cleaner — 5.4L (3V)

Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Disconnect the mass air flow (MAF) sensor electrical connector.
  1. Release the clamps and remove the air cleaner tray.
  1. Reach into the opening of the air cleaner assembly and depress the locking tabs on the air cleaner intake pipe and resonator to release the air cleaner intake pipe and resonator from the air cleaner assembly.
  1. Disconnect the crankcase ventilation tube quick connect coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Detach the generator wiring harness retainer from the air cleaner.
  1. Detach the vacuum hose retainer from the air cleaner.
  1. Remove the 4 bolts and the air cleaner.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).

  1.  CAUTION: The air cleaner-to-throttle body (TB) seal must be seated on the air cleaner prior to installation.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
