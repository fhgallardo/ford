2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 12/22/2005

Fender Splash Shield

Removal and Installation

  1. If equipped, remove the wheelhouse lip moulding.
  1. Remove the front bumper cover brace.
  1. Remove the fender splash shield screws.
  1. Remove the fender splash shield.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
