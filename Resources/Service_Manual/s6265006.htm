SECTION 206-05: Parking Brake and Actuation
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Parking Brake Cable — Rear, LH

Removal and Installation

  1. Relieve the parking brake cable tension. For additional information, refer to Parking Brake Cable Tension Release in this section.
  1. Release the intermediate cable from the LH rear cable from the cable equalizer.
  1. Release the front of the LH rear cable conduit at the frame bracket.
  1. Remove and discard the double cable clamp.
  1. Disconnect the LH rear parking brake cable from the rear parking brake levers and remove the cable from the vehicle.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Inspect the parking brake system for correct operation.
