SECTION 415-02: Antenna
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Antenna Base


Item Part Number Description
1 18813 Antenna mast
2 18A927 Antenna base cap
3 N610019 Antenna base screws (3 required)
4 18A984 Antenna base and cable

Removal and Installation

  1. Lower the glove compartment.
    • Press the side tabs and release the glove compartment.
  1. Disconnect the antenna lead-in cable from the antenna cable.
    • Push the antenna cable end and the grommet through the side cowl.
  1. Remove the antenna mast.

  1. NOTE: A new antenna base cap must be installed.

    Remove the antenna base cap.
  1. Remove the RH front fender splash shield. For additional information, refer to Section 501-02 .
  1. Remove the 3 screws and the antenna base and cable assembly.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
