SECTION 501-14: Handles, Locks, Latches and Entry Systems
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Exterior Front Door Handle

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the front door trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Position the watershield aside.
  1. If equipped, release the lock cylinder actuating rod.
  1. Open the clip and disconnect the exterior front door handle actuating rod from the front door latch.
  1. Remove the 2 exterior front door handle nuts.
    • To install, tighten to 7 Nm (62 lb-in).
  1. If equipped, disconnect the remote keyless entry (RKE) keypad electrical connector and disconnect the harness channel from the door.
  1. Remove the 2 exterior front door handle nut seals.
  1. Remove the 2 bolts and the exterior front door handle.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
