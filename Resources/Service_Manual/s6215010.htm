SECTION 501-05: Interior Trim and Ornamentation
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 01/20/2006

Door Trim Panel — Rear, Regular Cab


Item Part Number Description
1 W701930-S424 Rear door trim panel bolt
2 27406 LH/ 27407 RH Rear door trim panel

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear door trim panel bolt.
  1. Remove the rear door trim panel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
