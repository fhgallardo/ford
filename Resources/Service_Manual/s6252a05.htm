Removal

  1. Remove the axle shaft. For additional information, refer to Axle Shaft in this section.

  1. NOTE: If the axle shaft oil seal is leaking, the axle housing vent may be plugged with foreign material.

    NOTE: Use care to avoid damaging the axle shaft oil seal bore.

    Using a suitable seal remover, remove and discard the axle shaft oil seal.
  1. Inspect the rear wheel bearing and axle shaft for wear or damage.
  1. If necessary, using the special tools, remove the rear wheel bearing.

Installation

  1. If removed, lubricate the new rear wheel bearing with axle lubricant.
  1. If removed, use the special tools to install the rear wheel bearing.
  1. Lubricate the lip of the new axle shaft oil seal with grease.
  1. Using the special tools, install the new axle shaft oil seal.
  1. Install the axle shaft. For additional information, refer to Axle Shaft in this section.
