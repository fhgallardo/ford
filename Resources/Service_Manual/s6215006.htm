SECTION 501-05: Interior Trim and Ornamentation
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 01/20/2006

B-Pillar Trim Panel — SuperCab

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the front door scuff plate.
  1. Remove the lower safety belt anchor bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the B-pillar trim cover.
  1. Remove the B-pillar bolt trim cover.
  1. Remove the B-pillar bolt.
  1. Remove the safety belt bolt cover.
  1. Remove the safety belt bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the rear seat backrest. For additional information, refer to Section 501-10 .
  1. Remove the pin-type retainer from the B-pillar trim panel.
  1. Pull the safety belt through the B-pillar trim panel.
  1. Remove the B-pillar trim panel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
