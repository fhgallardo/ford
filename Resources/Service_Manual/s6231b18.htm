Removal

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the LH inner fenderwell. For additional information, refer to Section 501-02 .
  1. Remove the 2 exhaust Y-pipe flange nuts and position the exhaust Y-pipe flange aside.
  1. Remove the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) system module-to-exhaust manifold tube.
  1. Remove and discard the 8 exhaust manifold nuts.
  1. Remove the LH exhaust manifold and discard the gasket.
  1. Remove and discard the 8 exhaust manifold-to-cylinder head studs.
  1. Inspect the exhaust manifold for flatness. For additional information, refer to Section 303-00 .

Installation

  1.  CAUTION: Do not use metal scrapers, wire brushes, power abrasive discs or other abrasive means to clean the sealing surfaces. These may cause scratches and gouges resulting in leak paths. Use a plastic scraper to clean the sealing surfaces.

    Clean the sealing surfaces with metal surface prep.
  1. Install 8 new exhaust manifold-to-cylinder head studs.
    • Tighten to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).

  1. NOTE: Install a new exhaust manifold gasket and new nuts.

    Position the gaskets and the LH exhaust manifold and tighten the nuts in the sequence shown.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Install the EGR system module-to-exhaust manifold tube.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Position the Y-pipe flange and install the 2 nuts.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Install the LH inner fenderwell. For additional information, refer to Section 501-02 .
