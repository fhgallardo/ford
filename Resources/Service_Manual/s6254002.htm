SECTION 205-04: Front Drive Halfshafts
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Front Drive Halfshafts

New halfshaft components are available as follows:

  • A boot repair kit which includes the boot, clamps, snap ring, retaining ring and grease.
  • A new inboard CV joint (plunge type).
  • A new outboard CV joint and interconnecting shaft assembly includes the outboard front wheel halfshaft joint (fixed type), boot, clamps and the front wheel excluder seal (assembled on the joint).
