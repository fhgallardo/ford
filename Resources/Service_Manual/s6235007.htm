SECTION 303-05: Accessory Drive
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Accessory Drive Belt Idler Pulley — 4.2L

Removal and Installation

  1. Rotate the tensioner counterclockwise and detach the drive belt from the idler pulley.
  1. Remove the bolt and the belt idler pulley.
    • To install, tighten to 55 Nm (41 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
