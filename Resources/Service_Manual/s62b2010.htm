Removal

NOTE: New O-ring seals must be installed any time the power steering lines are disconnected from the steering gear.

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the 4 skid plate bolts and the skid plate, if equipped.

  1. NOTE: Do not allow the steering column shaft to rotate while the lower shaft is disconnected or damage to the clockspring can result. If there is evidence that the shaft has rotated, the clockspring must be removed and recentered. For additional information, refer to Section 501-20B

    Remove the bolt and disconnect the lower steering column shaft.
  1. Remove the 2 oil drip shield bolts and the drip shield.

  1. NOTE: New O-ring seals must be installed any time the lines are disconnected from the steering gear.

    Remove the power steering line clamp plate bolt and disconnect the power steering high pressure line.
    • Discard the O-ring seal.
  1. Disconnect the power steering return line.
    • Discard the O-ring seal.
  1. Loosen the 2 outer tie-rod end lock nuts.
  1. Remove the 2 outer tie-rod end nuts.

  1. NOTE: Note the number of times the outer tie-rod ends turn for reference during assembly.

    Using the special tool, separate the outer tie-rod ends from the wheel knuckles and remove the outer tie-rod ends.
  1. Remove the 2 steering gear bracket-to-crossmember nuts.
  1. Remove the 2 steering gear bracket-to-crossmember bolts.
  1. Remove the 2 steering gear bracket-to-gear nuts.
  1. Remove the 2 steering gear bracket-to-gear bolts.
  1. Remove the steering gear.
    • Remove the steering gear through the LH wheel opening.

Installation

  1. Install new O-ring seals on the power steering lines.
  1. Install the steering gear.
    • Install the steering gear through the LH wheel opening.
  1. Install the 2 steering gear bracket-to-gear bolts and nuts.
    • Tighten to 250 Nm (185 lb-ft).
  1. Install the 2 steering gear bracket-to-crossmember bolts and nuts.
    • Tighten to 103 Nm (76 lb-ft).
  1. Install the 2 outer tie-rod ends on the steering gear.
  1. Position the 2 outer tie-rod ends on the wheel knuckles and install the nuts.
    • Tighten to 115 Nm (85 lb-ft).
  1. Tighten the 2 outer tie-rod end lock nuts.
    • Tighten to 103 Nm (76 lb-ft).
  1. Connect the power steering high pressure line and install the power steering line clamp plate bolt.
    • Tighten to 23 Nm (17 lb-ft).
  1. Position the oil drip shield and install the 4 bolts.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Install the skid plate and the 4 skid plate bolts, if equipped.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Fill the power steering system. For additional information, refer to Section 211-00 .
    • Check and, if necessary, align the front end. For additional information, refer to Section 204-00 .
