2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Roof Opening Panel Shield

Removal and Installation

NOTE: The roof opening panel motor must be initialized when repairs are carried out on any part of the roof opening panel, including: whenever the roof opening panel motor has been removed from the roof opening panel assembly, a new roof opening panel motor has been installed or when a new roof opening panel frame has been installed. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Motor Initialization in this section.

  1. Remove the roof opening panel glass. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Glass in this section.
  1. Slide the roof opening panel shield halfway forward.
  1. Disengage the roof opening panel shield guide feet.
    1. Push the roof opening panel shield toward one side of the vehicle.
    1. Lift the front portion of the roof opening panel shield upward to disengage the guide feet.
  1. Slide the roof opening panel shield forward and release the rear guide feet.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
  1. Initialize the roof opening panel motor. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Motor Initialization in this section.
