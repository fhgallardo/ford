SECTION 413-13: Parking Aid
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Parking Aid Module


Item Part Number Description
1 14A005 Parking aid module electrical connectors (part of 14A005)
2 W707142 Parking aid module nuts (2 required)
3 15K866 Parking aid module

Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the parking aid module electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the 2 nuts and the parking aid module.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
