SECTION 307-01: Automatic Transmission — 4R70E/4R75E
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
IN-VEHICLE REPAIR
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

1-2 Accumulator

Removal

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Drain transmission fluid and remove the transmission fluid pan and filter. For additional information, refer to Fluid Pan, Gasket and Filter .
  1. Compress the 1-2 accumulator piston and remove the accumulator piston retaining ring.
  1. Remove the 1-2 shift accumulator assembly.
    1. Remove the 1-2 accumulator cover.
    1. Remove the inner and outer 1-2 accumulator springs.
    1. Remove the 1-2 accumulator piston.
    1. Remove the 1-2 accumulator upper spring.

Installation

  1. Install the 1-2 shift accumulator assembly.
    1. Install the 1-2 accumulator upper spring.
    1. Install the 1-2 accumulator piston.
    1. Install the inner and outer 1-2 accumulator springs.
    1. Install the 1-2 accumulator cover.
  1. Compress the 1-2 accumulator piston and remove the accumulator piston retaining ring.
  1. Install the transmission fluid pan and filter. For additional information, refer to Fluid Pan, Gasket and Filter .
