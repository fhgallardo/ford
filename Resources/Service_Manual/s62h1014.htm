SECTION 417-01: Exterior Lighting
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Fog Lamp

NOTE: LH side shown, RH side similar


Item Part Number Description
1 W706599 Fog lamp bracket bolts (3 required)
2 15266 Fog lamp bracket
3 15200/15201 Fog lamp assembly (RH/LH)
4 — Fog lamp adjuster screw and spring (part of 15200)

Removal and Installation

NOTE: Make sure the fog lamp switch and ignition switch are in the OFF position.

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Disconnect the fog lamp electrical connector.
  1. Detach the fog lamp wiring harness from the fog lamp bracket.
  1. Remove the 3 bolts and the fog lamp bracket.
    • Remove the adjuster screw and spring.
  1. Release the tabs and remove the fog lamp assembly.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Adjust the fog lamps. For additional information, refer to Front Fog Lamp Adjustment in this section.
