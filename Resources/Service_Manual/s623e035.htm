SECTION 303-14: Electronic Engine Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 02/22/2006

Variable Camshaft Timing (VCT) Oil Control Solenoid — 5.4L (3V)

NOTE: LH shown, RH similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 14A464 Variable camshaft timing (VCT) oil control solenoid electrical connector
2 6C535 Grommet
3 6C260 VCT oil control solenoid bolt
4 6B297 VCT oil control solenoid

Removal and Installation

Both sides

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .

LH side

  1. Remove the 3 bolts and position the power steering fluid reservoir aside.
    • To install, tighten to 11 Nm (8 lb-ft).

Both sides

  1. Disconnect the variable camshaft timing (VCT) oil control solenoid electrical connector.
  1. Remove the grommet.
  1. Remove the bolt and the VCT oil control solenoid.
    • To install, tighten to 5 Nm (44 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
