SECTION 501-03: Body Closures
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
GENERAL PROCEDURES
Procedure revision date: 02/02/2005

Door Alignment — Rear, Regular and SuperCab

All adjustments

  1. Remove the upper rear door striker cover.
  1. Remove the upper rear door striker.
  1. Remove the lower rear door striker.

Horizontal and vertical adjustments

  1. Loosen the rear door hinge-to-rear door bolts.
  1. Adjust the rear door.
  1. Tighten the rear door hinge-to-rear door bolts to 30 Nm (22 lb-ft).

Fore and aft adjustment

  1. Remove the corner trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Loosen the rear door hinge-to-body nuts.
  1. Adjust the door.
  1. Tighten the rear door hinge-to-body nuts to 30 Nm (22 lb-ft).
  1. Install the corner trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .

All adjustments

  1. Install the lower rear door striker.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Install the upper rear door striker.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Install the upper rear door striker cover.
