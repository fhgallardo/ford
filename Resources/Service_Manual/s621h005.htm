Roof Opening Panel Alignment — Horizontal Adjustment

NOTE: The roof opening panel motor must be initialized when repairs are carried out on any part of the roof opening panel, including: whenever the roof opening panel motor has been removed from the roof opening panel assembly, a new roof opening panel motor has been installed or when a new roof opening panel assembly has been installed. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Motor Initialization in this section.

  1. Loosen the 2 roof opening panel glass screws on the driver side.

  1.  CAUTION: Use care to avoid scratching the roof or damaging the roof opening panel seal.

    Insert a plastic shim (or similar tool) with a thickness of approximately 3 mm (0.11 in) between the rear of the roof opening panel seal and the roof opening panel.
  1. Gently move the roof opening panel forward.
  1. Tighten the roof opening panel glass screws.
  1. Repeat for the passenger side.
  1. Using a 0.5 mm (0.02 in) feeler or suitable gauge, inspect seal contact around the entire roof opening panel glass.
  1. Initialize the roof opening panel motor. For additional information, refer to Roof Opening Panel Motor Initialization in this section.
