SECTION 412-01: Climate Control
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Mode Door Actuator — Air Inlet Door


Item Part Number Description
1 W701696 Air inlet mode door actuator screw (3 required)
2 19E616 Air inlet mode door actuator
3 18B574 Air inlet mode door actuator electrical connector

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the instrument panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-12 .
  1. Disconnect the air inlet mode door actuator electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 3 air inlet mode door actuator screws.
  1. Remove the air inlet door mode door actuator.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
