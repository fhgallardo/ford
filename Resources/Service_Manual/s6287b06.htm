SECTION 308-07B: Transfer Case
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
IN-VEHICLE REPAIR
Procedure revision date: 02/02/2005

Transfer Case Rear Output Shaft Seal


Special Tool(s)
Installer, Input Shaft Oil Seal
308-249 (T96T-7127-A)

Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .

  1. NOTE: Index-mark the rear driveshaft yoke and pinion flange to maintain initial driveshaft balance during installation.

    Remove the rear driveshaft. For additional information, refer to Section 205-01 .
  1. Using a suitable tool, remove the transfer case rear output shaft seal.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Using the special tool, install the oil seal.
