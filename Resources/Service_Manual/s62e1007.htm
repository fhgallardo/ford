2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Battery Tray

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the battery. For additional information, refer to Battery in this section.
  1. Release the 2 wiring harness locators from the battery tray.
  1. If equipped, unclip the vacuum reservoir and position it aside.
  1. Remove the 4 bolts and the battery tray.
    • To install, tighten to 17 Nm (13 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
