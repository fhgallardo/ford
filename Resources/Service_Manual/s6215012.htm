REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

Door Trim Panel — Rear, SuperCrew

NOTE: LH side shown, RH side similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 25514 RH/ 25515 LH Rear window garnish moulding
2 14B134 RH/ 14B135 LH Window control switch panel
3 14A005 Window switch electrical connector
4 224A60 Rear door handle cover plate
5 N602704-S301 Rear door handle screw (2 required)
6 264B52 RH/ 264B53 LH Rear door handle
7 W505424-S Trim panel bolt
8 W701930-S424 Rear door trim panel bolt (3 required)
9 27406 RH/27407 LH Rear door trim panel

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear window garnish moulding.
  1. Remove the window control switch panel.
    • Disconnect the window control switch electrical connector.
  1. Remove the rear door handle cover plate.
  1. Remove the 2 rear door handle screws.
  1. Remove the rear door handle.
    • Guide the door handle through the opening in the trim panel.
  1. Remove the trim panel bolt.
  1. Remove the 3 rear door trim panel bolts.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not pull directly outward on the rear door trim panel. Damage may occur.

    Remove the rear door trim panel.
    • Lift upward to remove the rear door trim panel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
