SECTION 412-01: Climate Control
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Blower Motor Speed Control


Item Part Number Description
1 19E624 Blower motor speed control
2 W701696 Blower motor speed control screw
3 18B574 Blower motor speed control electrical connector

Removal and Installation

  1. NOTE: The blower motor speed control can be accessed from below the RH side of the instrument panel.

    Disconnect the blower motor speed control electrical connector.
  1. Remove the blower motor speed control screw.
  1. Detach the blower motor speed control.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
