SECTION 501-10: Seating
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Seat Cushion Cover — Rear

Removal and Installation

All seats

  1. Remove the rear seat cushion. For additional information, refer to Seat — Rear in this section.
  1. Remove the 4 rear seat lower anchors and tethers for children (LATCH) bolts and the LATCH.
    • To install, tighten to 6 Nm (53 lb-in).
  1. Remove the 60/40 retention clip.
  1. Remove the center support riser.

60 percent seat

  1. Remove the 60 percent support riser bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 60 percent support riser pivot bolt and bushing.
    • To install, tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the left support riser.
  1. Remove the 60 percent seat cushion latch handle screw and handle.
  1. Remove the 60 percent seat cushion latch cover screw and cover.
  1. Remove the 60 percent seat latch cushion bolt and latch.
    • To install, tighten to 30 Nm (22 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: Use care when separating the seat upholstery from the hook-and-loop strip, or the hook-and-loop strip can be torn from the seat cushion foam pad.

    Separate the hook-and-loop fasteners.
  1. Remove the 60 percent cushion trim cover.
    • Remove the hog rings and remove the seat trim cover.

40 percent seat

  1. Remove the 40 percent support riser bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 40 percent support riser pivot bolt, bushing and right support riser.
    • To install, tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 40 percent seat cushion latch handle screw and handle.
  1. Remove the 40 percent seat cushion latch cover screw and cover.
  1. Remove the 40 percent seat cushion latch bolt and latch.
    • To install, tighten to 30 Nm (22 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: Use care when separating the seat upholstery from the hook-and-loop strip, or the hook-and-loop strip can be torn from the seat cushion foam pad.

    Separate the hook-and-loop fasteners.
  1. Remove the 40 percent trim cover.
    • Remove the hog rings and remove the seat trim cover.

All seats

  1. To assemble, reverse the disassembly procedure.
