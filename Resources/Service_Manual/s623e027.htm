SECTION 303-14: Electronic Engine Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 02/02/2005

Knock Sensor (KS) — 5.4L (3V)


Item Part Number Description
1 14A464 RH knock sensor (KS) electrical connector
2 W500225 RH KS bolt
3 12A699 RH KS
4 14A464 LH KS electrical connector
5 W500225 LH KS bolt
6 12A699 LH KS

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the intake manifold. For additional information, refer to Section 303-01C .
  1. Disconnect the knock sensor (KS) electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the bolts and the 2 KS.
    • To install, tighten to 20 Nm (15 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
