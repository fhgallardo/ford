Removal

NOTE: The following procedure is applicable for all of the front axle mounting bushings.

  1. NOTE: It is not necessary to remove the axle assembly from the jack.

    Remove the axle assembly. For additional information, refer to Axle Assembly in this section.
  1. Position the carrier bushing removal tools.
    1. Position the front axle mounting bushing cup remover.
    1. Position the Drawbolt through front axle mounting bushing cup remover and the bushing.
    1. Thread the Bushing Remover onto the Drawbolt.
  1. Tighten the Drawbolt to remove the carrier bushing.

Installation

  1. Position the carrier bushing and the carrier bushing installation tools.
    1. Position the Ring Gauge on the outer face of the bushing flange.
    1. Position the Front Axle Mounting Bushing Collet Installer over the Ring Gauge and the bushing flange.
    1. Slide the Front Axle Mounting Bushing Cup Remover over the Front Axle Mounting Bushing Collet Installer.
    1. Insert the Drawbolt.
    1. Thread the Front Axle Mounting Bushing Collet Installer onto the Drawbolt.

  1. NOTE: The Ring Gauge will correctly locate the carrier bushing when the Front Axle Mounting Bushing Collet Installer bottoms out on the carrier.

    Tighten the Drawbolt until the bushing collet installer contacts the carrier.
    • Remove the tools.
  1. Install the axle assembly. For additional information, refer to Axle Assembly in this section.
