Removal and Installation

  1. Using a suitable suction device, drain the power steering fluid reservoir.
  1. Remove the 3 fluid reservoir bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 11 Nm (8 lb-in).
  1. Release the clamp and disconnect the return line from the fluid reservoir.
  1. Release the clamp and disconnect the suction line from the fluid reservoir.
  1. Remove the power steering fluid reservoir.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Fill the power steering system. For additional information, refer to Section 211-00 .
