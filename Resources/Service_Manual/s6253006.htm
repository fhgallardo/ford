SECTION 205-03: Front Drive Axle/Differential
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Axle Assembly

Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. If equipped, remove the front skid plate nuts and the front skid plate.
    • To install, tighten to 15 Nm (11 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the front driveshaft. For additional information, refer to Section 205-01 .

  1.  CAUTION: Do not allow the halfshaft to hang unsupported.

    Remove the 12 halfshaft flange-to-axle shaft flange bolts, and disconnect the front halfshafts from the axle shafts.
    • To install, tighten to 82 Nm (60 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 4 crossmember bolts and the crossmember.
    • To install, tighten to 90 Nm (66 lb-ft).
  1. Use a high-lift jack to support the axle assembly.
  1. Remove the axle tube bushing nut and axle tube bushing bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 115 Nm (85 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the lower mounting bushing bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 115 Nm (85 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the upper mounting bushing bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 115 Nm (85 lb-ft).
  1. Carefully lower the axle assembly.
  1. Disconnect the vent hose from the axle vent.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
