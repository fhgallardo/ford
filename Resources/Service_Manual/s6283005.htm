SECTION 308-03: Manual Transaxle/Transmission
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL
Procedure revision date: 03/23/2006

Transmission


Special Tool(s)
Clutch Coupling Tool
308-182 (T88T-70522-A)
High-Lift Transmission Jack
164-R3508 or equivalent
  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Unclip the console cover, then slide the cover and boot assembly up.
  1. Remove the gearshift lever nut.
  1. Install the gearshift lever nut on the LH side of the lever, then tighten the nut to remove the eccentric stud out of the gearshift lever. Remove the upper gearshift lever.
  1. Remove the 4 screws and the lower boot.
  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the 4 bolts and the lower gearshaft lever.
  1. If transmission disassembly is required, remove the drain plug and drain the transmission fluid.
    • Install the drain plug after draining all the fluid.
    • Tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the driveshaft. For additional information, refer to Section 205-01 .
  1. Disconnect the starter motor ground wire, then remove the 2 starter motor bolts and the starter motor. Using mechanic's wire, position the starter motor aside.
    • To install, tighten to 33 Nm (24 lb-ft).
  1. Disconnect the wire harness from the transmission and disconnect both of the heated oxygen sensor (HO2S) electrical connectors.
  1. Disconnect the reverse lamp switch electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the vehicle speed sensor (VSS) electrical connector.
  1. Remove the front stabilizer bracket nuts and the brackets. For additional information, refer to Section 204-01A . Move the front stabilizer bar downward to position it out of the way.
  1. Remove the 2 nuts and disconnect the fuel lines and the wire harness from the rear of the transmission. Position them aside.
  1. Using the special tool, disconnect the clutch hydraulic line.
  1. Position a suitable jack under the transmission. Secure the transmission to the jack with a safety strap.
  1. Remove the 2 exhaust hanger bolts, and remove the hanger.
  1. Remove the 2 exhaust heat shield bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 15 Nm (11 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 4 crossmember bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 90 Nm (66 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 2 transmission mount nuts and the crossmember.
    • To install, tighten to 98 Nm (72 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the transmission mount from the transmission.
    • To install, tighten to 80 Nm (59 lb-ft).
  1. Lower the transmission enough to gain access to the upper transmission-to-engine bolts. Remove 9 transmission-to-engine bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 60 Nm (44 lb-ft).
  1. Pull the transmission rearward until the input shift is clear of the pressure plate, then lower the transmission from the vehicle.
