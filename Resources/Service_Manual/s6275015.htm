SECTION 307-05: Automatic Transaxle/Transmission External Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Selector Lever Knob

Removal

  1. Using a suitable tool, pry forward on the top of the selector lever button and remove the button.
  1. Remove the screw, slide the selector lever knob up and remove the knob.

Installation

  1.  CAUTION: Do not overtighten the screw or damage to the selector lever knob can occur.

    Position the selector lever knob on the selector lever and install the screw.
  1. Position the selector lever button in place and press in on the top of the button to install.
