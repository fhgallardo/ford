Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Disconnect the LH front and rear heated oxygen sensor (HO2S) electrical connectors.
  1. Using the special tool, remove the 2 LH HO2S.
    • To install, tighten to 46 Nm (34 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 4 exhaust manifold-to-catalytic converter nuts.
    • To install, tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the exhaust support bracket from the crossmember.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the LH dual converter Y-pipe assembly aside.

  1. NOTE: Install new exhaust manifold nuts.

    Remove and discard the 8 nuts and remove the exhaust manifold.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft) in the sequence shown.
  1. Remove and discard the exhaust manifold gaskets.

  1. NOTE: Install new exhaust manifold-to-cylinder head studs.

    Remove and discard the 8 exhaust manifold-to-cylinder head studs.
    • To install, tighten to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: Do not use metal scrapers, wire brushes, power abrasive discs or other abrasive means to clean the sealing surfaces. These may cause scratches and gouges resulting in leak paths. Use a plastic scraper to clean the sealing surfaces.

    Clean the sealing surfaces with metal surface prep. Follow the directions on the packaging.
  1. Inspect the exhaust manifold for flatness. For additional information, refer to Section 303-00 .
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
