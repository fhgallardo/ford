SECTION 307-05: Automatic Transaxle/Transmission External Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Transmission Selector Lever — Column Shift

Removal

  1. Remove the steering wheel. For additional information, refer to Section 211-04 .
  1. Remove the screw and the lower steering column shroud.
  1. Remove the 2 screws and the upper steering column shroud.
  1. Disconnect the overdrive cancel switch electrical connector.
  1. Remove the retaining screw and remove the shift lever.

Installation

  1. Position the shift lever in the steering column and install the screw.
    • Tighten to 20 Nm (15 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: Route the overdrive cancel switch wire harness carefully to prevent damage to the wire harness.

    Connect the overdrive cancel switch electrical connector.
  1. Position the upper steering column shroud and install the 2 screws.
  1. Position the lower steering column shroud and install the screw.
  1. Install the steering wheel. For additional information, refer to Section 211-04 .
