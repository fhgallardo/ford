Anti-Lock Control

Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS)

The anti-lock brake system consists of the following components:

  • Hydraulic control unit (HCU)
  • Anti-lock brake system (ABS) module
  • Rear wheel speed sensor
  • Rear wheel speed sensor ring
  • Front wheel speed sensors
  • Front wheel speed sensor rings
  • Yellow ABS warning indicator
  • Stoplamp switch
  • Traction control switch (TCS) (PCM controlled)

The front wheel speed sensor rings are integral to the front hub and bearing assemblies. Refer to Section 204-01A for rear wheel drive (RWD) or Section 204-01B for four wheel drive (4WD).

The rear wheel speed sensor ring is integral to the rear axle. Refer to Section 205-02A , Section 205-02B or Section 205-02C .
