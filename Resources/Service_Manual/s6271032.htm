Draining, all vehicles using a suitable flush and fill machine

 CAUTION: Always refer to the instructions supplied with the flush and fill machine.

NOTE: Draining fluid from the transmission by removing only the fluid pan is acceptable for normal or severe duty fluid maintenance.

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Use a suitable flush and fill machine to change the fluid.
  1. When connecting the flush and fill machine, connect the machine to the fluid cooler tube after the fluid cooler on the cooler return line. This will help remove any foreign material trapped in the fluid coolers.

Refill

  1. Use only clean automatic transmission fluid.
  1. Once the fluid exchange has been completed, disconnect the flush and fill machine. Reconnect any disconnected fluid cooler tubes.
  1. With the engine running and the transmission at normal operating temperature 66-77°C (150-170°F), check and adjust the transmission fluid level and check for any leaks. If fluid is needed, add fluid in increments of 0.24-liter (0.5-pint) until the correct level is achieved (fluid should be in the cross-hatched area of the fluid level indicator).

Draining, all vehicles not using a suitable flush and fill machine

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Loosen the transmission fluid pan bolts and allow the fluid to drain.
  1. After the fluid has drained, remove the transmission fluid pan.
  1. Do not remove the fluid filter. It is not necessary to change the fluid filter during a normal maintenance fluid change.
  1. Clean and inspect the transmission fluid pan, transmission fluid pan gasket and magnet.
  1. Thoroughly flush the cooler tubes. For additional information, refer to Transmission Fluid Cooler — Backflushing and Cleaning in this section.

Refill

  1. Position the magnet into the transmission fluid pan.

  1. NOTE: The fluid pan gasket is reusable, clean and inspect for damage. If not damaged, the gasket should be reused.

    Install the fluid pan and gasket.
    1. Position the fluid pan with the gasket in place.
    1. Install the bolts.
      • Tighten to 14 Nm (10 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: The use of any other transmission fluid can result in the transmission failing to operate in a normal manner or transmission failure.

    Fill the transmission.
    • Add 4.7 liters (5 qts) of clean automatic transmission fluid to the transmission through the fluid filler tube.
  1. Start the engine. Move the transmission range selector lever through all the gear ranges, checking for engagements.
  1. With the engine running and the transmission at normal operating temperature 66-77°C (150-170°F), check and adjust the transmission fluid level and check for any leaks. If fluid is needed, add fluid in increments of 0.24-liter (0.5-pint) until the correct level is achieved (fluid should be in the cross-hatched area of the fluid level indicator).
