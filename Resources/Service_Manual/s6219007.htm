SECTION 501-09: Rear View Mirrors
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 01/10/2005

Exterior Mirror Glass

Removal

NOTE: If equipped with power folding mirrors, they must be synchronized any time the mirrors have been folded or unfolded without using the mirror control switch. Refer to Exterior Mirror Motor Synchronization — Power Folding in this section.

  1. Position exterior mirror glass in the full UP position.
  1. Locate the notch at the bottom of the exterior mirror glass.
  1. Release the exterior mirror glass.
    1. Insert a long flat-blade screwdriver in the notch.
    1. Push the screwdriver into the notch until the glass is released.
  1. Remove the exterior mirror glass.
    • If equipped with heated mirrors, disconnect electrical connectors from the exterior mirror glass.

Installation

  1. If equipped with heated mirrors, connect the electrical connector to the mirror glass.
  1. Position the exterior mirror glass on the mirror motor.

  1.  CAUTION: Place a clean shop towel between hand and exterior mirror glass to prevent injury in the event of glass breakage.

    Place a hand against the back of the exterior mirror housing and a hand flat against the exterior mirror glass. Press the mirror glass in place until it is fully seated.
