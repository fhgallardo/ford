SECTION 501-10: Seating
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Seat Cushion Cover — Front, 20 Percent

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the 20 percent seat cushion. For additional information, refer to Seat — Front, 20 Percent in this section.
  1. Release the J-clips and remove the seat cushion pan.
  1. Remove the 6 hog rings (3 shown) and remove the 20 percent seat trim cover from the seat cushion foam pad.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
