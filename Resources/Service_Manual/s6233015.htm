SECTION 303-03: Engine Cooling
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 07/14/2005

Cooling Fan — Blade, Clutch and Shroud


Special Tool(s)
Wrench, Fan Clutch Nut
303-240 (T84T-6312-D)
Holding Wrench, Fan Pulley
303-239 (T84T-6312-C)


Item Part Number Description
1 8A616 Cooling fan assembly
2 N807658 Cooling fan shroud bolts (2 required)
3 8146 Cooling fan shroud



Item Part Number Description
4 57632 Fan clutch bolts (4 required)
5 8600 Cooling fan
6 8A616 Fan clutch

All vehicles

  1. Remove the pin-type retainers and the radiator appearance cover.

Vehicles equipped with a 4.2L or 4.6L (2V) engine

  1. Remove the air cleaner outlet pipe. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .

Vehicles equipped with a 5.4L (3V) engine

  1. Remove the air cleaner intake pipe. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Remove the bolts and the air cleaner bracket.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).

Vehicles equipped with a 4.6L (2V) or 5.4L (3V) engine

  1. Detach the battery cable harness from the radiator shroud.

All vehicles

  1. Using the special tools, remove the cooling fan assembly and position it in the fan shroud.
    • To install, tighten to 55 Nm (41 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the bolts and the fan shroud and fan.
    • To install, tighten to 6 Nm (53 lb-in).
  1. If necessary, remove the bolts and separate the fan and the fan clutch.
    • To install, tighten to 17 Nm (13 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
