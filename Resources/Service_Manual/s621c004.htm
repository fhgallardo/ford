SECTION 501-12: Instrument Panel and Console
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Steering Column Cover

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the steering wheel. For additional information, refer to Section 211-04 .
  1. Remove the lower steering column cover screw.
  1. Remove the lower steering column cover.
  1. Remove the 2 upper steering column cover screws.
  1. Remove the upper steering column cover.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
