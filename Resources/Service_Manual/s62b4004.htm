SECTION 211-04: Steering Column
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 05/24/2006

Steering Wheel


Special Tool(s)
Remover, Differential Bearing
205-116 (T77F-4220-B1)

Removal and Installation

 CAUTION: Removing the steering wheel without using a puller can damage the column bearings.

  1. Place the steering wheel in the straight-ahead position and turn the ignition switch to the OFF position.
  1. Remove the driver air bag module. For additional information, refer to Section 501-20B .
  1. Disconnect the steering wheel switches electrical connector.

  1. NOTE: Do not remove the bolt until the steering wheel has been separated from the column.

    Loosen the steering wheel bolt.
  1. Using the special tool, separate the steering wheel from the steering column assembly.
  1. Remove the steering wheel bolt and the steering wheel.
    • To install, tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
