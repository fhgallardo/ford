Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .

  1. NOTE: New O-ring seals must be installed any time the lines are disconnected from the steering gear.

    Remove the power steering line clamp plate bolt, rotate the clamp plate and disconnect the return line.
    • Discard the O-ring seal.
    • To install, tighten to 23 Nm (17 lb-ft)
  1. Release the clamp, disconnect the return line from the power steering fluid cooler and remove the line.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Fill the power steering system. For additional information, refer to Section 211-00 .
