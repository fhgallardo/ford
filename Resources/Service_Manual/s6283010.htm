SECTION 308-03: Manual Transaxle/Transmission
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DISASSEMBLY AND ASSEMBLY OF SUBASSEMBLIES
Procedure revision date: 02/17/2006

Reverse Idler Gear

Disassembly


Item Part Number Description
1 7140 Reverse idler gear shaft
2 7N037 Thrust washer
3 7E139 Reverse idler gear needle bearing
4 7141 Reverse idler gear
5 7N037 Thrust washer
6 7156 Retaining ring
  1. Disassemble the reverse idler gear as follows:
    • Retaining ring
    • Thrust washer
    • Reverse idler gear
    • Two reverse idler gear needle bearings
    • Thrust washer
  1. Inspect all reverse idler gear components for wear or damage. Install new components as necessary.

Assembly

  1. Assemble the reverse idler gear as follows:
    • Thrust washer
    • Two reverse idler gear needle bearings
    • Reverse idler gear
    • Thrust washer
    • Retaining ring
  1. Measure reverse idler gear end play.
    • If end play is not within specifications, install a retaining ring of correct thickness.
    • Reverse idler gear retaining rings:
      • 1.5 mm (0.0590 in)
      • 1.6 mm (0.0629 in)
      • 1.7 mm (0.0669 in)
      • 1.8 mm (0.0708 in)
      • 1.9 mm (0.0748 in)
