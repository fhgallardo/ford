SECTION 501-09: Rear View Mirrors
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 08/15/2006

Auto-Dimming Interior Mirror


Special Tool(s)
Mirror Remover
501-D118


Item Part Number Description
1 — Interior mirror electrical connector (part of 14A005)
2 19A548 Electronic compass sensor
3 17700 Interior mirror

Removal

  1. Remove the wire cover.
  1. Disconnect the electrical connector.

  1. NOTE: The compass (if equipped) does not need to be removed prior to this step.

    NOTE: High level mirror shown, others similar.

    Insert the special tool between the mirror mount and windshield bracket from the top.
  1. If equipped, separate the compass from the mirror assembly by disengaging the compass locking tabs.
  1. Release the mirror mount from the windshield bracket by pulling the tool and mirror down and away from the windshield.

Installation

  1. Slide the mirror mount over the windshield bracket from the top and adjust the mirror to the up position.
  1. Quickly press the mirror assembly downward along the windshield until a click is felt. The click indicates the base is engaged to the windshield bracket.
  1. If equipped, install the compass, electrical connector and wire cover. Check the compass zone and calibration. For additional information, refer to Section 413-08 .
