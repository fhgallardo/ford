SECTION 501-11: Glass, Frames and Mechanisms
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Window Regulator and Motor — Rear Door, SuperCrew

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear door trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Remove the rear door mounted speaker. For additional information, refer to Section 415-03 .
  1. Remove the rear door interior moulding.
  1. Remove the rear door watershield.
  1. Secure the rear door glass in the full upward position with tape.

  1. NOTE: Some vehicles may use rivets instead of rear door window glass-to-regulator assembly bolts.

    Remove the rear door window glass-to-regulator assembly bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 11 Nm (8 lb-ft).
  1. Disconnect the rear door window motor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the rear door window regulator assembly bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 11 Nm (8 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the rear door window regulator assembly.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
