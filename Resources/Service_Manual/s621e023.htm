Door Latch Remote Control — SuperCab


Material
Item Specification

ESB-M1C93-B

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear door trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Position the watershield aside.
  1. Remove the 3 upper door latch bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 3 latch remote control assembly bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 3 lower door latch bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).
  1. Release the actuating rod from the rear door interlock.
  1. Remove the latch remote control assembly.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Lubricate the upper and lower rear door latches after installation.
