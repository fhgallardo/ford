Removal and Installation

All vehicles

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable and wait at least one minute. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. If equipped, remove the rear entertainment system. For additional information, refer to Section 415-07 .
  1. If equipped with a single overhead console, remove the overhead console. If equipped with a modular overhead console, remove the modular components. For additional information, refer to Section 501-12 .
  1. Remove the A-pillar trim panel. For additional information, refer to A-Pillar Trim Panel in this section.
  1. Remove the B-pillar trim panel. For additional information, refer to B-Pillar Trim Panel — Regular Cab , B-Pillar Trim Panel — SuperCab or B-Pillar Trim Panel — SuperCrew in this section.
  1. If equipped, remove the C-pillar trim panel. For additional information, refer to C-Pillar Trim Panel in this section.
  1. Disconnect the rear electrical connector.
  1. Remove the LH and RH sun visor retaining screws.
  1. Remove the LH and RH sun visor retaining clips.
  1. Remove the sun visors.
    • If equipped, disconnect the electrical connectors.
  1. Disconnect the interior mirror electrical connector.
  1. If equipped, remove the roof opening panel trim.
  1. Remove the 2 coat hooks.
  1. If equipped, remove the 2 headliner pin-type retainers.
  1. If equipped, remove the 4 modular overhead console track screws.

Regular Cab and SuperCab

  1. Remove the LH and RH rear door latch cover bolts.
  1. Remove the LH and RH rear door latch covers.
  1. Remove the headliner.
    • Slide the headliner forward until it detaches from the roof.

SuperCrew

  1. Position the headliner aside.
  1. Remove the rear window glass. For additional information, refer to Section 501-11 .
  1. Remove the headliner.
    • Remove the headliner through the rear window glass opening.

All vehicles

  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Place the headliner in position by aligning the headliner and rails with the front overhead console bracket.
