Removal and Installation

All vehicles

  1. Remove the headlamps. For additional information, refer to Section 417-01 .

  1. NOTE: The front bumper cover-to-front bumper bolts must be installed new.

    Remove the front bumper cover-to-front bumper bolts.
  1. Remove the hood over-slam bumper bolts.
  1. Remove the hood over-slam bumpers.
  1. Remove the front bumper cover brace bolts.
  1. Remove the front bumper cover braces.
  1. Remove the front bumper cover-to-front fender bolts.
  1. Remove the front bumper cover pin-type retainers.

Vehicles equipped with wheelhouse lip mouldings

  1. Remove the 4 retaining nuts.
  1. Remove the fender splash shield pin-type retainers.
  1. Remove the 4 fender splash shield screws.
  1. Position aside the wheelhouse lip mouldings.
    • Disconnect the 6 wheelhouse lip moulding clips.
  1. Release the retaining clips.

All vehicles

  1. Remove the bumper cover bolts located at the headlamp openings.
  1. Remove the front bumper cover.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
