SECTION 415-01: Audio Unit
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Subwoofer Amplifier


Item Part Number Description
1 — Subwoofer amplifier electrical connector
2 MA147A10 Subwoofer amplifier screws (4 required)
3 18C808 Subwoofer amplifier

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the speaker enclosure. For additional information, refer to Section 415-03 .
  1. Disconnect the subwoofer amplifier electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 4 screws and the subwoofer amplifier.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
