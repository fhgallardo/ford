SECTION 502-02: Full Frame and Body Mounting
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 10/03/2005

Transmission Support Crossmember


Item Part Number Description
1 W707959-S426 Heat shield screws (2 required)
2 N621945-S Transmission mount nuts (2 required)
3 636800 Transmission support crossmember bolts (4 required)
4 6A023 Transmission support crossmember

Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the 2 exhaust hanger bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 2 heat shield screws.
    • To install, tighten to 20 Nm (15 lb-ft).
  1. Support the transmission with a suitable jack.
  1. Remove the 2 transmission support nuts.
    • To install, tighten to 103 Nm (76 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 4 transmission support crossmember bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 115 Nm (85 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the transmission support crossmember.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
