SECTION 501-16: Wipers and Washers
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Windshield Wiper Motor

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the wiper pivot arms. For additional information, refer to Wiper Pivot Arm in this section.
  1. Remove the cowl grille panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-02 .
  1. Disconnect the windshield wiper motor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 3 windshield wiper motor assembly bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
  1. Remove the windshield wiper motor assembly.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
