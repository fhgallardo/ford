SECTION 307-05: Automatic Transaxle/Transmission External Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Brake Shift Interlock Actuator — Column Shift

Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the steering wheel. For additional information, refer to Section 211-00 .
  1. Remove the 2 screws and the lower dash panel.
  1. Remove the screw and the lower steering column shroud.
  1. Remove the 2 screws and the upper steering column shroud.
  1. Disconnect the clockspring electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the screw and slide the multi-function switch up and position it aside.
  1. Place a piece of tape on the clockspring to prevent it from rotating.
  1. Press the 3 tabs on the clockspring and slide the clockspring off.
  1. Remove the 3 screws and remove the clockspring and shroud mounting bracket.
  1. Disconnect the brake shift interlock switch electrical connector.
  1. Release the tab and slide the brake shift interlock down to remove.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
