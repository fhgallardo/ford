SECTION 412-01: Climate Control
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Temperature Blend Door Actuator


Item Part Number Description
1 18B574 Temperature blend door actuator electrical connector
2 W701696 Temperature blend door actuator screw (3 required)
3 19E616 Temperature blend door actuator

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the audio unit. For additional information, refer to Section 415-01 .
  1. While pushing the glove compartment door tab, position the glove compartment downward.
  1. Remove the 3 temperature blend door actuator screws.
  1. Detach the temperature blend door actuator.
  1. Disconnect the temperature blend door actuator electrical connector.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
