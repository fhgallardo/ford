 WARNING: The correct protective equipment and clothing must be worn when using paints, solvents, sanding or grinding. Proper ventilation must also be provided to avoid harmful fumes in the work area.

 CAUTION: Protect adjacent areas/substrates when preparing for and during refinishing.

NOTE: Peeling/delamination concerns can be described as lack of adhesion, either between the substrate and topcoats or between individual coats of paint.

  1. Wash the repair area with pH-neutral soap and water.
  1. Remove any trim, emblems and hardware from the area to be repaired.

  1. NOTE: All delamination must be removed.

    Sand or media blast the damaged surface, keeping the repair area as minimal as possible.
  1. Treat any bare metal surface to prevent flash corrosion, and prime and block sand as necessary prior to refinishing.
  1. Mask the adjacent panels to protect from overspray.
  1. Spot repair the base coat as necessary, following the paint manufacturer's prescribed procedures.
  1. Following the paint manufacturer's prescribed procedure, apply clear coat to the entire panel.
