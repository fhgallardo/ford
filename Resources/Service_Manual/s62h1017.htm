SECTION 417-01: Exterior Lighting
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Light Sensor


Item Part Number Description
1 15046B63 Defroster grille panel
2 W707628 Light sensor screws
3 14A597 Light sensor
4 14401 Light sensor electrical connector

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the defroster grille panel.
  1. Remove the light sensor screws.
  1. Disconnect the electrical connector.
  1. Remove the light sensor.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
