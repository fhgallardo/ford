Removal and Installation

All vehicles

  1. Remove the hood release retaining bolt.
  1. Position aside the hood release assembly.
    • Disconnect the hood release cable routing retainer.

  1. NOTE: The front bumper cover-to-front bumper bolts must be installed new.

    Remove the 3 front bumper cover-to-front bumper bolts.
  1. Remove the hood-over slam bumper bolts.
  1. Remove the hood-over slam bumpers.

Early build Mark LT

  1. Remove the 3 arm dam pin-type retainers.

Late build Mark LT and all F-150

  1. Remove the 13 air dam retaining bolts..

All vehicles

  1. If equipped, disconnect the fog lamp electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the 4 bumper mounting bracket-to-front bumper nuts.
    • To install, tighten to 115 Nm (85 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Transfer components as necessary.
