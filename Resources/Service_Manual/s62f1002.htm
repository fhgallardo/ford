SECTION 415-01: Audio Unit
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Audio System

Normal Operation

Audio Unit

The audio is enabled when the key is in RUN or ACC. The following audio units are available:

  • AM/FM with clock
  • AM/FM single compact disc (CD)
  • AM/FM dual-media (CD/cassette)
  • AM/FM 6-disc CD changer with MP3

Optional Equipment

The following options are available:

  • Steering wheel controls
    • Allows control over the audio unit functions through a set of switches located on the steering wheel.
  • Digital versatile disc (DVD) player
    • Enabled when the key is in RUN or ACC.
    • Mounted on the overhead rail system.
    • Includes infra-red headphones and a remote control.
  • Satellite radio receiver
    • Allows reception of digitally broadcast radio stations from a satellite.
    • Includes its own antenna.
