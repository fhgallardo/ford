Removal

 WARNING: The safety belt pretensioner is a pyrotechnic device. Always wear safety glasses when repairing an air bag equipped vehicle and when handling a safety belt buckle pretensioner or safety belt retractor pretensioner. Never probe a pretensioner electrical connector. Doing so could result in pretensioner or air bag deployment and could result in personal injury.

 WARNING: To reduce the risk of personal injury, do not use any memory saver devices.

NOTE: If a seat equipped with a supplemental restraint system (SRS) component is being serviced, the SRS must be depowered .

NOTE: The air bag warning lamp illuminates when the restraints control module (RCM) fuse is removed and the ignition switch is ON. This is normal operation and does not indicate a supplemental restraint system (SRS) fault.

NOTE: The SRS must be fully operational and free of faults before releasing the vehicle to the customer.

NOTE: Driver seat shown, passenger seat similar.

  1. Move the seat fully rearward.
  1. Remove the 2 front seat track-to-floor front bolts.
  1. Move the seat to the most forward position.
  1. Depower the SRS. For additional information, refer to Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) Depowering and Repowering in the General Procedures portion of Section 501-20B .
  1. Remove the 2 front seat track-to-floor rear bolts.

  1. NOTE: Additional component connectors may exist due to vehicle options.

    Disconnect the electrical connector(s).
  1. Remove the seat from the vehicle.

Installation

  1. Position the seat in the vehicle.

  1. NOTE: Additional component connectors may exist due to vehicle options.

    Connect the electrical connector(s).
  1. Install the 2 front seat track-to-floor rear bolts.
    • Tighten the rear seat bolts to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft) in the following sequence:
      • Rear outboard bolt.
      • Rear inboard bolt.
  1. Repower the SRS. For additional information, refer to Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) Depowering and Repowering in the General Procedures portion of Section 501-20B .
  1. Move the seat fully rearward.
  1. Install the 2 front seat track-to-floor bolts.
    • Tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
