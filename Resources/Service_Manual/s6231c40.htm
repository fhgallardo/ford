All vehicles

  1. Using a suitable floor crane, position the engine assembly into the vehicle.

  1.  CAUTION: Only use hand tools when installing the LH engine mount bolt or damage to the engine mount can occur.

    Apply Threadlock 262 to the bolt threads and install the LH engine support insulator bolt.
    • Tighten to 350 Nm (258 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: Make sure the RH engine support insulator mating surfaces and the washer mating surface are free of foreign material and corrosion before installation. Install a new washer.

     CAUTION: Only use hand tools when installing the RH engine mount nuts or damage to the engine mount can occur.

    Apply Threadlock 262 to the stud threads and install the RH engine support insulator washer and nuts.
    • Tighten to 250 Nm (184 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the special tool.

Manual transmission vehicles

  1. Install the clutch. For additional information, refer to Section 308-01 .

Automatic transmission vehicles

  1. NOTE: The upper 2 transmission-to-engine bolts will be installed later.

    Install the lower 5 transmission-to-engine bolts.
    • Tighten to 60 Nm (44 lb-ft).
  1. Position the shift cable bracket and install the bolts and connect the shift cable.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Install 4 new torque converter-to-flexplate nuts.
    • Tighten to 36 Nm (27 lb-ft).
  1. Install the cylinder block opening cover.
  1. Install the flexplate inspection cover and the 2 bolts.
    • Tighten to 34 Nm (25 lb-ft).
  1. Install the starter. For additional information, refer to Section 303-06 .
  1. Position the transmission cooler tube support bracket and install the bolt.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).

All vehicles

  1. If equipped, connect the block heater electrical connector.

  1. NOTE: LH shown, RH similar.

    Connect the LH and RH heated exhaust gas oxygen sensor (HO2S) electrical connector and attach the electrical connector retainer.
  1. Attach the starter electrical harness support bracket.
  1. Connect the engine oil temperature (EOT) sensor electrical connector.
  1. Position the A/C compressor and install the 3 bolts.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Install the 4 exhaust manifold flange nuts.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).

Automatic transmission vehicles

  1. Install the upper 2 transmission-to-engine bolts.
    • Tighten to 60 Nm (44 lb-ft).
  1. Position the transmission filler tube and install the bolt.
    • Tighten to 20 Nm (15 lb-ft).

All vehicles

  1. Connect the crankshaft position (CKP) sensor wiring harness retainer to the starter motor wiring harness.
  1. Connect the generator wiring harness retainer to the RH cylinder head.
  1. Connect the A/C compressor and the A/C high pressure cut-off switch electrical connectors.
  1. Connect the generator wiring harness retainer to the RH cylinder head.
  1. Position the power steering pump assembly and install the 3 bolts.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Using the special tool, install a new O-ring seal on the pressure line fitting.
  1. Connect the power steering pressure tube.
    • Tighten to 65 Nm (48 lb-ft).
  1. Using the special tool, install the power steering pump pulley.
  1. Position the power steering pressure hose support bracket and install the nut.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Position the power steering reservoir assembly and install the bolt.
    • Tighten to 23 Nm (17 lb-ft).
  1. Using a new O-ring seal, connect the A/C manifold and tube assembly and install the nut.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Position the A/C manifold and tube assembly support bracket and install the nut.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Connect the heater hose.
  1. Disconnect the coolant hose.
  1. Connect the electrical connector and position the ground strap and install the bolt.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Position the cowl extension panel and install the bolts.
  1. Attach the wiring harness and the windshield washer hose retainers to the cowl panel extension.
  1. Install the intake manifold. For additional information, refer to Intake Manifold in this section.
  1. Install the radiator. For additional information, refer to Section 303-03 .
  1. Install the powertrain control module (PCM). For additional information, refer to Section 303-14 .
  1. Install the cowl. For additional information, refer to Section 501-02 .
  1. Install the hood.
  1. Fill the crankcase with clean engine oil.
  1. Evacuate and charge the A/C system. For additional information, refer to Section 412-00 .
  1. Fill and bleed the power steering system. For additional information, refer to Section 211-00 .
