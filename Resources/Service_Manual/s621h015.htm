SECTION 501-17: Roof Opening Panel
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 10/06/2006

Roof Opening Panel Control Switch


Item Part Number Description
1 18519A58 Overhead console
2 14489 Overhead console electrical connectors
3 14529 Roof opening panel control switch

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the overhead console.
  1. Disconnect the 2 overhead console electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the 4 screws and light assembly from the overhead console.
  1. Disengage the clips and remove the switch from the overhead console.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
