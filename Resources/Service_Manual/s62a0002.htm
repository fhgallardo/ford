2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Fuel System

The vehicle:

  • is equipped with a multiport fuel injection (MFI) system.
  • has separately controlled fuel injectors mounted to the intake manifold for each cylinder.
  • has a fuel rail which is supplied with pressurized fuel from the fuel delivery module which is enabled by the fuel pump driver module through the powertrain control module (PCM).
  • has fuel injectors which are supplied with pressurized fuel from the fuel delivery rail.
  • fuel rail is equipped with a fuel pressure sensor.
  • uses an electronic returnless fuel system.
  • fuel pressure sensor controls the pressure going to the fuel injectors.
  • has an inertia fuel shutoff (IFS) switch, located behind the passenger side right foot kick panel, which shuts off the fuel in the event of a collision.
