SECTION 308-07B: Transfer Case
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Transfer Case

  1. Install a new transfer case-to-transmission gasket.
  1. Fill the transfer case. For additional information, refer to Transfer Case Draining and Filling in this section. Refer to Specifications in this section for the correct type and quantity of fluid.
  1. Tighten the transfer case-to-transmission bolts to 47 Nm (35 lb-ft) evenly in a star pattern.
