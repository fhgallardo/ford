Removal

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the accessory drive belt. For additional information, refer to Section 303-05 .
  1. Remove the fan blade assembly. For additional information, refer to Section 303-03 .
  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Using the special tool to prevent the crankshaft rotation, remove the crankshaft pulley bolt and washer.
  1. Using the special tools, remove the crankshaft pulley.

Installation

  1. Apply a bead of silicone gasket and sealant to the keyway in the crankshaft damper and use the special tool to install the crankshaft damper.
  1. Using the special tool to prevent crankshaft rotation, install the bolt.
    • Tighten to 160 Nm (118 lb-ft).
  1. Connect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Install the fan blade assembly. For additional information, refer to Section 303-03 .
  1. Install the drive belt. For additional information, refer to Section 303-05 .
