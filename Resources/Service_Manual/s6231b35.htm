Disassembly

  1. Release the 2 coolant hose clamps and remove the coolant hose.
  1. Remove the 4 throttle body (TB) bolts and the TB.
    • Discard the TB gasket.
  1. Remove the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) system module bolt.
  1. Remove the EGR system module stud bolt and the EGR system module.
    • Discard the EGR system module gasket.
  1. Remove the 4 TB spacer bolts and the TB spacer.
    • Discard the TB spacer gasket.
  1. Remove the 4 fuel rail bolts.
  1. Remove the fuel rail.
  1. Remove the 8 fuel injectors
    • Discard the 16 fuel injector O-ring seals.
  1. Remove the 8 lower intake manifold bolts and the separate the upper and lower intake manifolds.
    • Discard the lower intake manifold gasket.

Assembly

  1. Install a new lower intake manifold gasket and install the upper to the lower intake manifold.
  1. Install the 8 bolts and tighten the lower intake manifold bolts in 2 stages.
    • Stage 1: Tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
    • Stage 2: Tighten an additional 90 degrees (1/4 turn).
  1. Install the new 16 fuel injector O-ring seals and lubricate the O-ring seals with clean engine oil and install the fuel injectors into the upper intake manifold.
  1. Install the fuel rail and the 4 fuel rail bolts.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Install a new throttle body spacer gasket and the throttle body spacer and tighten throttle body spacer bolts in 2 stages.
    • Stage 1: Tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
    • Stage 2: Tighten an additional 90 degrees (1/4 turn).
  1. Install a new EGR gasket, with the raised gasket surface facing the throttle body spacer, and the EGR system module and the stud bolt.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Install the EGR system module bolt.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Install a new throttle body gasket and the throttle body and tighten the 4 throttle body bolts in 2 stages.
    • Stage 1: Tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
    • Stage 2: Tighten an additional 90 degrees (1/4 turn).
  1. Install the coolant hose and the 2 coolant clamps.
