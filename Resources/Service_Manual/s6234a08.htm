SECTION 303-04A: Fuel Charging and Controls — 4.2L
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Fuel Charging Wiring Harness


Item Part Number Description
1 14A624 Fuel charging wiring harness-to-main engine wiring harness electrical connector
2 14489 Intake manifold runner control actuator electrical connector
3 14A464 Fuel injector electrical connectors (6 required)
4 14A163 Wiring harness retainers (2 required)
5 14A464 Fuel rail pressure sensor electrical connector
6 9D930 Fuel charging wiring harness

Removal and Installation

 WARNING: Do not smoke or carry lighted tobacco or open flame of any type when working on or near any fuel-related components. Highly flammable mixtures are always present and may be ignited. Failure to follow these instructions may result in personal injury.

  1. Remove the upper intake manifold. For additional information, refer to Section 303-01A .
  1. Disconnect the fuel charging wiring harness-to-main engine wiring harness electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the intake manifold runner control (IMRC) actuator electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the 6 fuel injector electrical connectors.
  1. Disconnect the 2 wiring harness retainers.
  1. Remove the wire tie.
  1. Disconnect the fuel rail pressure sensor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the fuel charging wiring harness.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
