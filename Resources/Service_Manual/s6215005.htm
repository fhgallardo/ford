SECTION 501-05: Interior Trim and Ornamentation
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

B-Pillar Trim Panel — Regular Cab

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the front door scuff plate.
  1. Remove the pin-type retainers from the rear trim panel.
  1. Remove the rear trim panel.
  1. Remove the B-pillar bolt cover.
  1. Remove the B-pillar bolt.
  1. Remove the B-pillar trim panel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
