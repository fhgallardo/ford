SECTION 412-01: Climate Control
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Blower Motor


Item Part Number Description
1 W701696-S437 Blower motor screw (3 required)
2 19C603 Blower motor electrical connector
3 19805 Blower motor
4 18504 Blower motor wheel
5 18A287 Blower motor wheel clip

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the RH lower A-pillar trim panel.
  1. Position aside the carpet below the blower motor.
  1. Disconnect the blower motor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 3 blower motor screws.
  1. Remove the blower motor.
  1. Remove the blower motor wheel.
    1. Remove the blower motor wheel clip.
    1. Remove the blower motor wheel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
