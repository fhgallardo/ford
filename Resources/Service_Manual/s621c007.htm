SECTION 501-12: Instrument Panel and Console
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Instrument Cluster Center Finish Panel

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the 2 steering column opening trim screws.
  1. Remove the steering column opening trim.
  1. Remove the steering column cover. For additional information, refer to Steering Column Cover in this section.
  1. Remove the instrument panel center finish panel. For additional information, refer to Instrument Panel Center Finish Panel in this section.
  1. Remove the 2 instrument cluster finish panel screws.
  1. Remove the instrument cluster finish panel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
