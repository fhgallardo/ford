Removal

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the differential housing cover. For additional information, refer to Differential Housing Cover in this section.
  1. Remove the disc brake caliper bolts and position aside the disc brake caliper.
    • Using mechanic's wire, position aside the disc brake caliper.
  1. Remove the brake disc.
  1. Remove the differential pinion shaft.
    1. Remove and discard the differential pinion shaft lock bolt.
    1. Remove the differential pinion shaft.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not damage the rubber O-rings in the axle shaft grooves.

    Remove the U-washers.
    1. Push in on the axle shafts.
    1. Remove the 2 U-washers.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not damage the axle shaft oil seals.

    Remove the 2 axle shafts.

    • NOTE: If the Diagnostic Chart has directed the servicing of the differential clutch packs, the packs can be removed at this point. For additional information, refer to Differential Case and Ring Gear — Traction-Lok® in this section.

      Remove the differential gears by rotating the 2 differential pinion gears and washers to the differential case window.
    • Remove the 2 differential side gears and clutch packs and tag them "right" and "left".

Installation

  1. Lubricate the lip of the axle shaft oil seal with grease.

  1. NOTE: Install the left and right differential side gears and clutch packs, if removed.

    Position the 2 differential pinion gears and rotate them to align with the pinion shaft holes.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not damage the axle shaft oil seal.

    Install the 2 axle shafts.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not damage the rubber O-rings in the U-washer grooves.

    Install the U-washers.
    1. Position the 2 U-washers on the button end of the axle shafts.
    1. Pull the axle shafts outward.

  1. NOTE: If a new pinion shaft lock bolt is unavailable, coat the threads with threadlock prior to installation.

    Install the differential pinion shaft.
    • Align the hole in the differential pinion shaft with the differential case lock bolt hole.
    • Install a new differential pinion shaft lock bolt.
      • Tighten to 30 Nm (22 lb-ft).
  1. Install the brake disc.
  1. Install the disc brake caliper and the disc brake caliper bolts.
    • Tighten to 27 Nm (20 lb-ft).
  1. Install the differential housing cover. For additional information, refer to Differential Housing Cover in this section.
