Removal

  1. Remove the air cleaner outlet pipe and the air cleaner assembly. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Remove the power steering reservoir support bracket-to-intake manifold bolt.
  1. Remove the 2 power steering reservoir support bracket-to-cylinder head bolts and position the power steering reservoir and support bracket assembly aside.
  1. Remove the bolt and position the oil level indicator aside.
  1. Disconnect the quick connect coupling and remove the crankcase ventilation tube. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Remove the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) system module-to-exhaust manifold tube.
  1. Disconnect the 3 engine wiring harness retainers.
  1. Remove the 5 LH valve cover studs.
  1. Remove the 5 LH valve cover bolts.
  1. Remove the LH valve cover bolt.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not use metal scrapers, wire brushes, power abrasive discs or other abrasive means to clean the sealing surfaces. These tools cause scratches and gouges which make leak paths. Use a plastic scraping tool to remove all traces of old sealant.

    Remove the LH valve cover.
    • Clean the valve cover mating surface of the cylinder head with silicone gasket remover and metal surface prep. Follow the directions on the packaging.
    • Discard the valve cover gasket. Clean the valve cover gasket groove with soap and water or a suitable solvent.

Installation

  1. Apply instant gel adhesive completely around the gasket groove in the valve cover and install the new valve cover gasket.

  1. NOTE: If the valve cover is not secured within 4 minutes, the sealant must be removed and the sealing area cleaned with silicone gasket remover and metal surface prep. Follow the directions on the packaging. Allow to dry until there is no sign of wetness, or 4 minutes, whichever is longer. Failure to follow this procedure can cause future oil leakage.

    Apply a bead of silicone gasket and sealant in 2 places where the engine front cover meets the cylinder head.
  1. Install the valve cover and the 5 stud bolts and 6 bolts.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in) in the sequence shown.
  1. Connect the 3 engine wiring harness retainers.
  1. Install the EGR system module-to-exhaust manifold tube.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Position the crankcase ventilation tube and connect the quick connect coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Position the oil level indicator and install the bolt.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Position the power steering reservoir and support bracket assembly and install the 2 power steering reservoir support bracket-to-cylinder head bolts.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Install the power steering reservoir support bracket-to-intake manifold bolt.
    • Tighten to 11 Nm (8 lb-in).
  1. Connect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Install the air cleaner outlet pipe and the air cleaner assembly. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
