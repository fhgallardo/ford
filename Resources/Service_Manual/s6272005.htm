Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the 3 pushpins and position the air deflector aside.
  1. Using the special tool, disconnect the fluid cooler tubes from the fluid cooler.
  1. Remove the 2 bolts and remove the transmission fluid cooler.
    • To install, tighten to 15 Nm (11 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
