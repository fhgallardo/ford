SECTION 307-05: Automatic Transaxle/Transmission External Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 11/21/2005

Brake Shift Interlock Actuator — Floor Shift

Removal

  1. Remove the floor console left side kick panel.
  1. Lift the console storage lid cover and remove the console finish panel.
  1. Disconnect the transmission shift cable from the arm of the transmission floor shifter.
  1. Disconnect the transmission shift cable from the transmission shift cable bracket on the transmission floor shifter.
  1. Remove the transmission floor shifter screws.
  1. Disconnect the transmission floor shifter electrical connector and remove the shifter from the vehicle.
  1. Disconnect the electrical connector.
    1. Disconnect the overdrive cancel button electrical connector.
    1. Cut the tie strap holding the wire harness to the base of the shifter.
  1. Remove the brake shift interlock actuator.
    1. Remove the bulb from the housing
    1. Cut the tie strap from the wire harness.
    1. Remove the 2 screws and remove the brake shift interlock actuator.

Installation

  1. Install the brake shift interlock actuator.
    1. Install the brake shift interlock actuator and install the 2 screws.
    1. Install the bulb into the housing
    1. Install a new tie strap on the wire harness.
  1. Connect the electrical connector.
    1. Connect the overdrive cancel button electrical connector.
    1. Install a new tie strap to the wire harness.
  1. Position the transmission floor shifter assembly in the console and install the screws.
    • Tighten to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).
  1. Connect the transmission floor shift harness electrical connector.
  1. Install the transmission shift cable in the transmission floor shifter cable bracket.
  1. Connect the transmission shift cable to the transmission selector arm.
  1. Install the console finish panel.
  1. Install the left console kick panel.
