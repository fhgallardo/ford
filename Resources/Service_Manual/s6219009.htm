SECTION 501-09: Rear View Mirrors
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 08/15/2006

Interior Rear View Mirror


Special Tool(s)
Mirror Remover
501-D118
Installer, Rear View Mirror
501-025 (T94P-17700-AH)

Removal

  1.  CAUTION: Twisting the interior rear view mirror base from right to left may cause a stress fracture of the windshield.

    Remove the wire cover (if equipped).
  1. Remove the compass by pulling down on the compass rearmost edge (if equipped).
  1. Insert the special tool between the mirror mount and windshield bracket from the bottom.
  1. Release the mirror mount from windshield bracket by pulling the tool away from the windshield.

Installation

  1. Slide the mirror mount over the windshield bracket from the top and adjust the mirror to the up position.
  1. Using the special tool, press the mirror assembly downward along the windshield until the mount is seated on the windshield bracket.
  1. Install compass and wire cover (if equipped).
