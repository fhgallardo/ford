2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Parking Brake Warning Indicator Switch

Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-02 .
  1. Disconnect the parking brake warning indicator switch electrical connector.
  1. Remove the parking brake warning indicator switch screw and the switch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
