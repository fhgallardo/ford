SECTION 206-05: Parking Brake and Actuation
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
GENERAL PROCEDURES
Procedure revision date: 02/15/2006

Parking Brake Cable Tension Release

  1. Release the parking brake.
  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. With an assistant, release the parking brake cable tension by pulling down on the intermediate cable at the cable-to-cable union until the parking brake control sector rotates to its stop and a 4 mm (0.15 in) x 150 mm (5.9 in) retainer pin can be inserted.
  1. Disconnect the cable at the cable-to-cable union.
