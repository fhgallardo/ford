Transmission Draining and Filling


Material
Item Specification
MERCON® Multi-Purpose ATF Transmission Fluid
XT-2-QDX 		MERCON®
  1. Remove the drain plug and drain the transmission.
    • Position a suitable drain pan under the transmission.
  1. Clean and install the drain plug.
    • Tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).

  1. NOTE: Before removing, clean the area around the filler plug.

    Remove the filler plug.

  1. NOTE: Fill the transmission with the vehicle on a level surface.

    Using a suitable oil suction gun, fill the transmission to the correct level with the specified fluid. The fluid must be just below the fill plug hole.
    • Transmission capacity is 3.5 liters (3.1 quarts).
  1. Install the filler plug.
    • Tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
