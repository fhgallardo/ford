Removal

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the power steering line clamp plate bolt and rotate the plate.

  1. NOTE: New O-ring seals must be installed any time the lines are disconnected from the steering gear.

    Disconnect the high pressure line from the steering gear and discard the O-ring seal.
  1. Remove the high pressure line bracket-to-crossmember bolt.
  1. Remove the high pressure line bracket-to-engine nut and bolt.
  1. Disconnect the high pressure line fitting-to-pump and remove the high pressure line.
    • Remove and discard the Teflon® seal.

Installation

  1. Using the special tool, install a new Teflon® seal on the high pressure line fitting.
  1. Position the high pressure line and connect the high pressure line fitting-to-pump.
    • If equipped with 4.6L or 5.4L engine, tighten to 65 Nm (48 lb-ft).
    • If equipped with 4.2L engine, tighten to 20 Nm (15 lb-ft).
  1. Install the high pressure line bracket-to-engine nut and bolt.
    • If equipped with a 4.2L or 4.6L engine, tighten the nut to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).
    • If equipped with a 5.4L engine, tighten the nut to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the high pressure line bracket-to-crossmember bolt.
    • Tighten to 11 Nm (8 lb-ft).
  1. Install a new O-ring seal and position the high pressure line on the steering gear.
  1. Rotate the power steering line clamp plate install the bolt.
    • Tighten to 23 Nm (17 lb-ft).
  1. Fill the power steering system. For additional information, refer to Section 211-00 .
