SECTION 308-01: Clutch
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 05/01/2006

Clutch Disc and Pressure Plate


Special Tool(s)
Clutch Aligner
308-090 (T83T-7137-A)


Item Part Number Description
1 6375 Flywheel
2 7550 Clutch disc
3 7563 Clutch pressure plate
4 — Clutch pressure plate bolt
5 — Transmission

Removal

  1. Remove the transmission. For additional information, refer to Section 308-03 .
  1. If the original components are to be installed, index-mark the clutch pressure plate and the flywheel.
  1. Remove the clutch pressure plate bolts, clutch pressure plate and the clutch disc.

Installation

  1. Adjust the clutch pressure plate as follows:
    • Rotate the adjusting ring counterclockwise until the tension springs are compressed.
    • Hold the adjusting ring, then release the pressure on the clutch pressure plate fingers.
  1. Using the special tool, position the clutch disc on the flywheel.

  1. NOTE: If installing the original clutch pressure plate, use the index marks made during removal.

    Position the clutch pressure plate on the dowels, install the clutch pressure plate bolts and remove the special tool.
    • Tighten to 55 Nm (41 lb-ft).
  1. Install the transmission. For additional information, refer to Section 308-03 .
    • Before securing the transmission to the engine, install the hydraulic line to the clutch slave cylinder.
  1. Operate the vehicle to check clutch operation.
