SECTION 303-14: Electronic Engine Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 12/01/2005

Crankshaft Position (CKP) Sensor — 4.6L (2V) and 5.4L (3V)


Item Part Number Description
1 14A464 Crankshaft position (CKP) sensor electrical connector
2 N806155 CKP sensor bolt
3 6C315 CKP sensor

Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Remove the accessory drive belt.
  1. If equipped, remove the 2 bolts and position the power steering fluid cooler aside.
    • To install, tighten to 11 Nm (8 lb-ft).
  1. Loosen the A/C compressor bolts enough to slide the A/C compressor down 25 mm (1 in), to allow access to the crankshaft position (CKP) sensor.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Disconnect the CKP sensor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the bolt and the CKP sensor.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
