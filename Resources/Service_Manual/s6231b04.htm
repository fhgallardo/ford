Removal

 WARNING: Do not smoke or carry lighted tobacco or open flame of any type when working on or near any fuel-related component. Highly flammable mixtures are always present and may be ignited. Failure to follow these instructions may result in personal injury.

 WARNING: Fuel in the fuel system remains under high pressure even when the engine is not running. Before servicing or disconnecting any of the fuel lines or fuel system components, the fuel system pressure must be relieved to prevent accidental spraying of fuel, causing personal injury or a fire hazard.

  1. Disconnect the fuel supply hose spring lock coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Drain the cooling system. For additional information, refer to Section 303-03 .
  1. Remove the air cleaner outlet tube. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Release the upper radiator hose clamp and position the upper radiator hose aside.
  1. Release the heater hose clamp and remove and position the heater hose aside.
  1. Disconnect the vapor tube quick connect coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Disconnect the throttle position (TP) sensor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the power steering reservoir support bracket bolt.
  1. Remove the 2 power steering reservoir support bracket bolts and the power steering reservoir support bracket.
  1. Disconnect the injection pressure (IPR) sensor electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the IPR sensor vacuum connector.
  1. Remove the brake booster bracket nut and disconnect the brake booster tube and position aside the brake booster bracket and tube.
  1. Remove the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) system module-to-exhaust manifold tube.
  1. Disconnect the EGR system module vacuum and electrical connectors.
  1. Disconnect the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) tube quick connect coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Rotate the tensioner clockwise and remove the accessory drive belt.
  1. If equipped, disconnect the vacuum hose.
  1. Disconnect the electronic throttle control electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the throttle body (TB) spacer coolant hose from the intake manifold.
  1. Remove the 4 bolts and the TB and TB spacer as an assembly.
    • Discard the TB spacer gasket.
  1. Disconnect the generator electrical connector.
  1. Remove the generator battery cable nut and the generator battery cable.
  1. Release the generator wiring harness retainer.
  1. Remove the 4 generator support bracket bolts and the generator support bracket.
  1. Remove the 2 lower generator bolts and the generator.
  1. Disconnect the 8 fuel injector electrical connectors.
  1. Disconnect the 8 ignition coil electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the 8 ignition coil bolts and the 8 ignition coils.
  1. Release the PCV coolant hose clamp and remove the PCV coolant return hose.
  1. Remove the 2 thermostat housing bolts and the thermostat housing and discard the O-ring seal.
  1. Remove the thermostat.
  1. Detach the wiring harness from the left valve cover and position aside.
  1. Remove the 9 intake manifold bolts.
  1. Remove the intake manifold and discard the RH and LH intake manifold gaskets.

Installation

  1.  CAUTION: Do not use metal scrapers, wire brushes, power abrasive discs or other abrasive means to clean the sealing surfaces. These tools cause scratches and gouges which make leak paths. Use a plastic scraping tool to remove all traces of old sealant.

    Clean the mating surfaces of the cylinder head and the intake manifold with metal surface prep and silicone gasket remover. Follow the directions on the packaging.
  1. Install the intake manifold.
    1. Position the new intake manifold gaskets.
    1. Position the intake manifold.
    1. Loosely install the 9 intake manifold bolts.
  1. Tighten the intake manifold bolts in the sequence shown.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Attach the wiring harness to the left valve cover.
  1. Install the thermostat.
  1. Install the thermostat housing and a new thermostat housing O-ring seal.
  1. Install the 2 thermostat housing bolts.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Install the 8 ignition coil and the 8 ignition coils bolts.
    • Tighten to 6 Nm (53 lb-in).
  1. Connect the 8 ignition coil electrical connectors.
  1. Connect the 8 fuel injector electrical connectors.
  1. Install the generator and the 2 lower generator bolts.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Install the generator support bracket and the 4 generator support bracket bolts.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Attach the generator wiring harness retainer.
  1. Install the generator battery cable and the generator battery cable nut.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Connect the generator electrical connector.
  1. Position a new gasket, the TB assembly and install the 4 bolts.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Connect the electronic throttle control electrical connector.
  1. Connect the TB spacer coolant hose to the intake manifold.
  1. If equipped, connect the vacuum hose.
  1. Rotate the tensioner clockwise and install the accessory drive belt.
  1. Install the PCV coolant return hose and install the PCV coolant hose clamp.
  1. Connect the PCV tube quick connect coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Connect the fuel supply tube spring lock coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Connect the EGR system module vacuum and electrical connectors.
  1. Install the EGR system module-to-exhaust manifold tube.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Connect the brake booster tube and position the brake booster bracket and install the nut.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Connect the IPR sensor vacuum connector.
  1. Connect the IPR sensor electrical connector.
  1. Install the power steering reservoir support bracket and the 2 power steering reservoir support bracket bolts.
    • Tighten to 17 Nm (13 lb-ft).
  1. Install the power steering reservoir support bracket bolt.
    • Tighten to 11 Nm (8 lb-ft).
  1. Connect the TP sensor electrical connector.
  1. Connect the vapor tube quick connect coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Install the heater hose and install the heater hose clamp.
  1. Install the upper radiator hose and clamp.
  1. Install the air cleaner outlet tube. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Connect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Fill and bleed the engine cooling system. For additional information, refer to Section 303-03 .
