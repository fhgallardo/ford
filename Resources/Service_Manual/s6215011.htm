2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 01/20/2006

Door Trim Panel — Rear, SuperCab

NOTE: LH side shown, RH side similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 1625514 RH/ 1625515 LH Rear window garnish moulding
2 W701930-S424 Bolt (3 required)
3 14B134 RH/ 14B135 LH Rear window control switch panel
4 14635 Electrical connector
5 W505424-S Door handle bezel bolt
6 224A60 Rear door handle cover plate
7 W701704-S309 Rear door handle bolt (2 required)
8 264B52 RH/ 264B53 LH Rear door handle
9 1827406 RH/ 1827407 LH Rear door trim panel

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear window garnish moulding.
  1. Remove the 3 bolts.
  1. Remove the rear window control switch.
    • Disconnect the rear window control switch electrical connector.
  1. Remove the door handle bezel bolt.
  1. Remove the rear door handle cover plate.
  1. Remove the 2 rear door handle bolts.
  1. Remove the rear door handle.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not pull directly outward on the rear door trim panel, as damage may occur.

    Remove the rear door trim panel.
    • Lift upward and remove the rear door trim panel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
