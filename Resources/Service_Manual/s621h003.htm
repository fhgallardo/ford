Pinpoint Tests

Pinpoint Test A: The Roof Opening Panel Has Excessive Wind Noise

Normal Operation

When the roof opening panel glass is completely closed, it is flush with the roof panel.

    Possible Causes
  • Incorrect roof opening panel adjustment
  • Poorly fitting roof opening panel seal/weatherstrip
  • Incorrect installation of the roof opening panel and motor/module
  • Incorrect curvature of the air deflector

Pinpoint Test B: The Roof Opening Panel Leaks

Normal Operation

When the roof opening panel glass is completely closed, it is flush with the roof panel.

    Possible Causes
  • Incorrect roof opening panel adjustments
  • Damaged roof opening panel drain hoses
  • Damaged roof opening panel seal/weatherstrip
  • Damaged roof opening panel frame
  • Incorrect position of the roof opening panel glass
  • Roof opening panel rear trough guide
  • Sealant joints at frame (rail and track assembly) joints

Pinpoint Test C: The Roof Opening Panel Rattles

Normal Operation

When the roof opening panel glass is completely closed, it is flush with the roof panel. All components should be secured correctly.

Possible Causes

Rattling noises from the roof opening panel can be caused by any of the following loosely or incorrectly installed components:

  • Roof opening panel glass adjustment
  • Roof opening panel air deflector assembly
  • Roof opening panel water trough and guides
  • Roof opening panel lifter assemblies
  • Roof opening panel tracks
  • Roof opening panel shield (sunshade)
  • Roof opening panel assembly

Pinpoint Test D: The Roof Opening Panel Is Noisy During Operation

Normal Operation

The roof opening panel operates smoothly while opening and closing.

    Possible Causes
  • Obstructed or damaged roof opening panel
  • Obstructed or damaged roof opening panel lifter assemblies
  • Incorrect roof opening panel adjustment
  • Noisy roof opening panel motor/module assembly
  • Roof opening panel shield (sunshade)

Pinpoint Test E: The Roof Opening Panel Does Not Open Or Close

NOTE: The roof opening panel motor initialization procedure must be done when repairs are carried out on part of the roof opening panel system, including: any time the roof opening panel motor has been removed from the roof opening panel assembly, a new roof opening panel motor has been installed or when a new roof opening panel assembly has been installed.

Normal Operation

The roof opening panel uses a integrated motor and module to operate the roof opening panel. A new roof opening panel motor/module are installed as an assembly.

The roof opening panel motor/module is energized by voltage on circuit 400 (LB/BK) when the key is in the ACC or RUN position. The roof opening panel motor/module grounds circuit 1173 (BN/OG) to the roof opening panel switch. When the roof opening panel switch is pressed, the roof opening panel motor/module receives a ground signal to circuit 1174 (WH/RD) for OPEN or circuit 1175 (TN/RD) for CLOSE.

    Possible Causes
  • Fuse(s)
  • Open in circuits 400 (LB/BK), 57 (BK), 1173 (BN/OG), 1174 (WH/RD) or 1175 (TN/RD)
  • Inoperative roof opening panel switch
  • Inoperative roof opening panel motor/module assembly
  • Inoperative accessory delay relay
  • Roof opening panel motor not initialized
