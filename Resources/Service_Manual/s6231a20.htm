Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the bolt and the oil level indicator and tube.
  1. Disconnect the LH heated oxygen sensor (HO2S) electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 2 nuts and detach the dual converter Y-pipe.
  1. Remove the 2 nuts and the engine lifting eye.
  1. Remove the 6 nuts, the LH exhaust manifold and the gasket. Discard the nuts and the gasket.

  1. NOTE: Note the locations of the 2 long studs.

    Remove and discard the 6 LH exhaust manifold studs.

Installation

  1. Inspect the LH exhaust manifold gasket mating surfaces for flatness. For additional information, refer to Section 303-00 .

  1. NOTE: Make sure the 2 long studs are installed in the locations noted during removal.

    Install 6 new LH exhaust manifold studs.
    • Tighten to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).
  1. Position a new LH exhaust manifold gasket and the LH exhaust manifold and install 6 new nuts.
    • Tighten the nuts in the sequence shown to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Position the engine lifting eye and install the nuts.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Attach the dual converter Y-pipe and install the 2 nuts.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Connect the HO2S electrical connector.

  1. NOTE: Install a new O-ring seal on the oil level indicator tube. Lubricate the O-ring seal with clean engine oil.

    Position the oil level indicator and tube and install the bolt.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
