SECTION 303-06: Starting System
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
GENERAL PROCEDURES
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Starter Motor Drive Gear and Flywheel Ring Gear Inspection

  1. Check the wear patterns on the starter drive and the flywheel ring gear. If the wear pattern is not normal, install a new starter motor. For additional information, refer to Starter Motor — 4.2L or Starter Motor — 4.6L, 5.4L in this section.
  1. If the starter drive gear and the flywheel ring gear are not fully meshing or the gears are milled or damaged, install a new starter motor. For additional information, refer to Starter Motor — 4.2L or Starter Motor — 4.6L, 5.4L in this section. Install a new flywheel or flexplate. For additional information, refer to Section 303-01A , Section 303-01B or Section 303-01C .
