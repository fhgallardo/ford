Disassembly and Assembly

  1. Remove the steering column. For additional information, refer to Steering Column in this section.
  1. Remove the clockspring. For additional information, refer to Section 501-20B .
  1. Remove the 3 screws and the clockspring bracket.
  1. Disconnect the electrical connector and remove the brake shift interlock solenoid.

  1. NOTE: Front shown, side similar.

    Using a suitable tool, press the locking pins and turn the switches clockwise 90 degrees to remove the brake shift interlock harness.
  1. Using a suitable tool, remove and discard the tilt lever.
  1. Remove the ignition switch.
    • Release the 2 locking tabs.
  1. Remove the screw and the shift lever and boot.

  1. NOTE: A new tilt lever must be installed.

    To assemble, reverse the disassembly procedure.
