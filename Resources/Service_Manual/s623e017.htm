Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Disconnect the catalyst monitor sensor electrical connector.

  1. NOTE: If necessary, lubricate the catalyst monitor sensor with lock lubricant to assist in removal.

    Using the special tool, remove the catalyst monitor sensor.
    • To install, tighten to 46 Nm (34 lb-ft).

  1. NOTE: Apply anti-seize to the threads of the catalyst monitor sensor.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
