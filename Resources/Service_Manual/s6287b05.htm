SECTION 308-07B: Transfer Case
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
IN-VEHICLE REPAIR
Procedure revision date: 02/02/2005

Transfer Case Shift Lever

Removal and Installation

  1. Pry upward on consolette to release it from the floor clips.
  1. Remove the transfer case shift lever.
    1. Remove the shift lever boot screws.
      • To install, tighten to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).
    1. Remove the shift lever cover.
    1. Remove the bolt.
      • To install, tighten to 24 Nm (18 lb-ft).
    1. Remove the shift lever.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
