Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the battery ground. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Remove the air cleaner outlet pipe. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Remove the throttle body (TB). For additional information, refer to Throttle Body in this section.
  1. Remove the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) system module. For additional information, refer to Section 303-08 .
  1. Release the clamp and disconnect the brake booster vacuum hose.
  1. Disconnect the vapor tube quick connect coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Disconnect the crankcase vent hose quick connect coupling at the rear of the intake manifold. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Disconnect the crankcase vent hose quick connect coupling at the RH valve cover. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Disconnect the TB spacer vacuum hose.
  1. Release the clamp and remove and plug the coolant hose.
  1. Release the clamp and remove and plug the coolant hose.
  1. Remove the 4 TB spacer bolts and the TB spacer.
    • Discard the TB spacer gasket.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
