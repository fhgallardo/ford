SECTION 308-07A: Four Wheel Drive (4WD) Systems
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Transfer Case Shift Motor


Material
Item Specification
Multi-Purpose Grease XG-4 and/or XL-5
ESB-M1C93-B


Item Part Number Description
1 14A464 Connector
2 N800670-S Bolt (3 required)
3 — J-clip (part of 7G360)
4 7A443 Bolt
5 7G360 Transfer case shift motor
6 7288 Shifter shaft seal
  1. With the ignition in the ON position, switch the mode select switch (MSS) to 4H.
  1. Turn the ignition to the OFF position.
  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Disconnect the harness connector.
  1. Remove the bracket bolt and the 3 mounting bolts, then remove the transfer case shift motor.
    • Remove the shifter shaft seal.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Apply a coat of multi-purpose grease to the transfer case shift motor.
