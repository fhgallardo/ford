SECTION 501-17: Roof Opening Panel
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Air Deflector

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the 2 air deflector screws.
  1. Rotate the front of the air deflector up, and slide the pivot points toward the rear of the vehicle to release the air deflector from the retaining hooks.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
