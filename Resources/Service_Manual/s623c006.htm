SECTION 303-12: Intake Air Distribution and Filtering
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Air Cleaner — 4.2L

Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Loosen the clamp and disconnect the air cleaner outlet pipe from the air cleaner.
  1. Disconnect the mass air flow (MAF) sensor electrical connector. Detach the electrical connector retainer.
  1. Lift the air cleaner from the grommets and remove the air cleaner.

  1.  CAUTION: The air cleaner tray inlet rubber grommet must be seated flush against the inner fender prior to air cleaner tray installation.

    Remove the air cleaner tray inlet rubber grommet.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
