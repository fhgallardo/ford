SECTION 211-02: Power Steering
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 02/04/2005

Power Steering Pump Pulley


Special Tool(s)
Remover, Power Steering Pump Pulley
211-016 (T69L-10300-B)
Installer, Power Steering Pump Pulley
211-185 (T91P-3A733-A)

Removal

Vehicles with 5.4L engine

  1. Remove the air cleaner intake pipe. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .

Vehicles with 4.6L engine

  1. Remove the cooling fan and shroud. For additional information, refer to Section 303-03 .

Vehicles with 4.2L engine

  1. Remove the air cleaner outlet pipe. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .

All vehicles

  1. Rotate the accessory drive belt tensioner clockwise and remove the accessory drive belt from the power steering pump pulley.
  1. Using the special tool, remove the power steering pump pulley.

Installation

All vehicles

  1. NOTE: Make sure the pulley is flush with the end of the power steering pump shaft.

    Using the special tool, install the power steering pump pulley.
  1. Rotate the accessory drive belt tensioner and install the accessory drive belt on the power steering pump pulley. For additional information, refer to Section 303-05 .

Vehicles with 4.2L engine

  1. Install the air cleaner outlet pipe. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .

Vehicles with 4.6L engine

  1. Install the cooling fan and shroud. For additional information, refer to Section 303-03 .

Vehicles with 5.4L engine

  1. Install the air cleaner intake pipe. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
