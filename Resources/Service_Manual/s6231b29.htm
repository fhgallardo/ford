Removal

All vehicles

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Remove the air cleaner and the air cleaner outlet pipe and resonator. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Remove the generator. For additional information, refer to Section 414-02 .
  1. Remove the bolts and position the cooling fan shroud away from the radiator.
  1. Disconnect the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) system module-to-exhaust manifold tube upper fitting.
  1. Disconnect the brake booster vacuum hose.
  1. Disconnect the throttle position (TP) sensor electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the vapor tube quick connect coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Disconnect the EGR system module vacuum and electrical connectors.
  1. Disconnect the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) tube quick connect coupling at the rear of the intake manifold. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Disconnect the PCV tube quick connect coupling at the RH valve cover. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Disconnect the electronic throttle control electrical connector.
  1. Disconnect the throttle body (TB) spacer vacuum hose.
  1. Remove the 4 TB spacer bolts and position the TB assembly aside.
    • Discard the TB spacer gasket.
  1. Install the special tool.
  1. Remove the 4 exhaust manifold-to-catalytic converter nuts.
  1. Remove the exhaust system support bracket bolts from the crossmember.
  1. Remove the transmission mount nuts.

4WD vehicles

  1. Remove the 4 nuts and lower the front stabilizer bar.

RH engine support insulator

  1. Remove the starter. For additional information, refer to Section 303-06 .

LH engine support insulator

  1. Remove the oil level indicator and tube. For additional information, refer to Oil Level Indicator and Tube in this section.
  1. If removing the LH engine support insulator in a 4WD vehicle, index mark the front driveshaft flange to the front axle pinion flange.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not let the driveshaft hang unsupported.

    If removing the LH engine support insulator in a 4WD vehicle, disconnect and support the front driveshaft.
    • Remove the 4 bolts.
    • Support the front driveshaft.

All vehicles

  1. Remove the RH engine support insulator nuts and washer.
  1. Remove the LH engine support insulator bolt.
  1. Raise the engine.

RH engine support insulator

  1. Remove the 3 RH engine support insulator bracket bolts.
  1. Remove the 2 RH engine support insulator bracket-to-engine support insulator bolts.
  1. Remove the RH engine support insulator bracket and the RH engine support insulator.

LH engine support insulator

  1. Remove the 3 LH engine support insulator bracket bolts.
  1. Remove the 2 LH engine support insulator bracket-to-engine support insulator bolts.
  1. Remove the LH engine support insulator bracket and the LH engine support insulator.

Installation

LH engine support insulator

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator-to-bracket mating surfaces of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Install the LH engine support insulator bracket and the LH engine support insulator.
  1. Install the 2 LH engine support insulator bracket-to-engine support insulator bolts.
    • Apply Threadlock 262 to the bolt threads prior to installation.
    • Tighten to 72 Nm (53 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator bracket-to-cylinder block mating surfaces of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Install the 3 LH engine support insulator bracket bolts.
    • Apply Threadlock 262 to the bolt threads prior to installation.
    • Tighten to 63 Nm (46 lb-ft).

RH engine support insulator

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator-to-bracket mating surfaces of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Install the RH engine support insulator bracket and the RH engine support insulator.
  1. Install the 2 RH engine support insulator bracket-to-engine support insulator bolts.
    • Apply Threadlock 262 to the bolts threads prior to installation.
    • Tighten to 72 Nm (53 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator bracket-to-cylinder block mating surfaces of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Install the 3 RH engine support insulator bracket bolts.
    • Apply Threadlock 262 to the bolts threads prior to installation.
    • Tighten to 63 Nm (46 lb-ft).

All vehicles

  1. Lower the engine.

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator-to-frame mating surfaces of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Apply Threadlock 262 to the threads and install the LH engine support insulator bolt.
    • Tighten to 350 Nm (258 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: Make sure the RH engine support insulator mating surfaces and the washer mating surface are free of foreign material and corrosion before installation. Install a new washer.

    Apply Threadlock 262 to the stud threads and install the RH engine support insulator washer and nuts.
    • Tighten to 250 Nm (184 lb-ft).

LH engine support insulator

  1. If installing the LH engine support insulator in a 4WD vehicle, align the front driveshaft flange-to-front axle pinion flange at the index mark and install the bolts.
    • Tighten to 111 Nm (82 lb-ft).
  1. Install the oil level indicator and tube. For additional information, refer to Oil Level Indicator and Tube in this section.

RH engine support insulator

  1. Install the starter. For additional information, refer to Section 303-06 .

4WD vehicles

  1. Install the front stabilizer bar and the 4 nuts.
    • Tighten to 30 Nm (22 lb-ft).

All vehicles

  1. Install the transmission mount nuts.
    • Tighten to 103 Nm (76 lb-ft).
  1. Install the exhaust system support bracket bolts from the crossmember.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Install the 4 exhaust manifold-to-catalytic converter nuts.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Position the new TB spacer gasket and the TB assembly and install the 4 bolts.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Connect the TB spacer vacuum hose.
  1. Connect the electronic throttle control electrical connector.
  1. Connect the PCV tube quick connect coupling at the RH valve cover. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Connect the PCV tube quick connect coupling at the rear of the intake manifold. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Connect the EGR system module vacuum and electrical connectors.
  1. Connect the vapor tube quick connect coupling. For additional information, refer to Section 310-00 .
  1. Connect the TP sensor electrical connector.
  1. Connect the brake booster vacuum hose.
  1. Connect the EGR system module-to-exhaust manifold tube upper fitting.
    • Tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. Position the cooling fan shroud and install the bolts.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Install the generator. For additional information, refer to Section 414-02 .
  1. Install the air cleaner and the air cleaner outlet pipe and resonator. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Connect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
