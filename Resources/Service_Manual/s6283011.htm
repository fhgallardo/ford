2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DISASSEMBLY AND ASSEMBLY OF SUBASSEMBLIES
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Synchronizers

Disassembly and Assembly

  1. NOTE: Match-mark parts for assembly.

    Disassemble the synchronizer.
    1. Remove one retaining spring.
    1. Flip the synchronizer over and remove the opposite retaining spring.
    1. Remove the 3 insert keys.
    1. Remove the sleeve.
  1. Inspect all synchronizer components for wear and damage.
  1. To assemble, reverse the disassembly procedure.
