SECTION 307-01: Automatic Transmission — 4R70E/4R75E
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
IN-VEHICLE REPAIR
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Main Control Valve Body


Special Tool(s)
Gauge, Transmission Solenoid Connectors
307-426

Removal

  1. Drain transmission fluid and remove the transmission fluid pan and filter. For additional information, refer to Fluid Pan, Gasket and Filter .

  1.  CAUTION: Do not pull on the molded lead frame. This may cause damage to the connector ends. Carefully pry up on the locking tabs to disconnect the solenoids. Disconnect the molded lead frame from the solenoids.

    Disconnect the molded lead frame from the solenoids.
    1. Disconnect the shift solenoid SSA and SSB.
    1. Disconnect the torque converter clutch (TCC).
    1. Disconnect the electronic pressure control (EPC) solenoid.
    1. Disconnect the bulkhead inter-connector.
  1. Remove the manual control valve detent lever bolt and spring.
  1. Remove the 24 valve body-to-case bolts and the EPC bracket bolt.
  1. Remove the main control valve body and discard the pump outlet screen.

Installation

  1. NOTE: Make sure that the drive pin of the manual valve detent lever assembly engages the manual valve in the correct location prior to installing the bolts.

    Position the main control valve body gasket and main control valve body using 2 bolts as a guide.

  1. NOTE: The main control valve body bolts will be tightened in later steps.

    Loosely install the 11 long main control valve body bolts.

  1. NOTE: The main control valve body bolts will be tightened in later steps.

    Loosely install the 12 short main control valve body bolts.

  1. NOTE: Lubricate the EPC solenoid O-rings with clean automatic transmission fluid.

    Position the EPC solenoid bracket and loosely install the bolt.
  1. Install the manual control valve detent lever spring and loosely install the bolt.
  1. Tighten the bolts in the sequence shown.
    • Tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Inspect the lead frame for damage.
    • Using the special tool, check all lead frame solenoid connections. The gauge should fit tightly and not fall out after being inserted.
    • If the special tool passes through any lead frame connector pins or does not feel like it makes a good contact, install a new lead frame.
  1. Connect the molded lead frame to the solenoids.
    • Connect the bulkhead inter-connector by pressing it in place by hand and fully seating the connector in place.
    • Connect the EPC solenoid by pressing it in place by hand and fully seating the connector in place. Make sure that the terminals pass fully through the connector slots.
    • Connect the TCC by pressing it in place by hand and fully seating the connector in place. Make sure that the terminals pass fully through the connector slots.
    • Connect the shift solenoid SSA and SSB by pressing it in place by hand and fully seating the connector in place. Make sure that the terminals pass fully through the connector slots.
  1. Install the transmission filter and pan. For additional information, refer to Fluid Pan, Gasket and Filter in this section.
