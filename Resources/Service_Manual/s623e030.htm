SECTION 303-14: Electronic Engine Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor — 5.4L (3V)


Item Part Number Description
1 14A464 Mass air flow (MAF) sensor electrical connector
2 W505562 MAF sensor bolt (2 required)
3 12B579 MAF sensor

Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Disconnect the mass air flow (MAF) sensor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the bolts and the MAF sensor.
    • To install, tighten to 7 Nm (62 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
