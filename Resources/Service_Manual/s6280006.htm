GENERAL PROCEDURES
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Flywheel Check

  1. Check the flywheel surface for:
    • cracks.
    • heat checks or other damage.
    • scoring.
    • discoloration.
      • minor scratches or discoloration should be removed with a fine emery cloth.
