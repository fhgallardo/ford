SECTION 303-14: Electronic Engine Controls
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 02/02/2005

Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor — 4.2L


Item Part Number Description
1 9628 Air cleaner cover clamp
2 9A612 Air cleaner tray
3 14A464 Mass air flow (MAF) sensor electrical connector
4 14603 Grommet
5 9A628 Air cleaner inner cover
6 12A690 MAF sensor jumper harness
7 W705761 MAF sensor bolt (2 required)
8 12B579 MAF sensor

Removal and Installation

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Release the clamp and detach the air cleaner cover from the air cleaner tray.
  1. Lift the air cleaner tray from the grommets and remove the air cleaner tray.
  1. Disconnect the mass air flow (MAF) sensor electrical connector and detach the electrical connector retainer.
  1. Remove the grommet from the air cleaner cover and slide it down the wiring harness to allow the removal of the air cleaner inner cover and the MAF sensor.
  1. Detach the air cleaner inner cover and pull the inner cover and MAF sensor out of the air cleaner cover and disconnect the MAF sensor jumper wire.
  1. Remove the bolts and the MAF sensor.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
