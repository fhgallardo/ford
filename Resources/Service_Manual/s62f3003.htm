REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/19/2006

Door Speaker

Front Door Tweeter Speaker

Front Door Speaker

Item Part Number Description
1 1501848 Front door speaker trim panel
2 W710033 Speaker screws (4 required)
3 18808 Speaker
4 180103 Speaker electrical connector


Rear Door Speaker

Item Part Number Description
1 W706766 Rear speaker screws (4 required)
2 18808 Rear speaker
3 180103 Rear speaker electrical connector

Removal and Installation

Front door tweeter

  1. Remove the front door trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Remove the 2 screws and the tweeter speaker.

Front door

  1. Remove the front door speaker trim panel.
  1. Remove the 4 screws and the front door speaker.
    • Disconnect the electrical connector.

Rear door

  1. Remove the rear door trim panel. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .
  1. Remove the 4 screws and the rear door speaker.
    • Disconnect the electrical connector.

All speakers

  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
