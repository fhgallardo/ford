Air Conditioning (A/C) Clutch Air Gap Adjustment


Special Tool(s)
Holding Fixture, Compressor Clutch
412-103 (T95L-19703-AH)

NOTE: The A/C clutch air gap must be measured and adjusted, using the correct method, to make sure of correct A/C function. An A/C clutch air gap that is greater than specifications may reduce A/C compressor performance or cause the A/C compressor to be inoperative, and may cause premature wear of the A/C clutch disc and hub and A/C compressor pulley. An A/C clutch air gap that is less than specifications may cause the A/C compressor to operate continuously, or may cause premature wear of the A/C clutch disc and hub and A/C compressor pulley.

NOTE: It is not necessary to recover the refrigerant for A/C clutch air gap adjustment if the A/C compressor is not being removed from the vehicle.

  1. Measure the A/C clutch air gap at 3 equally spaced places between the A/C clutch disc and the A/C compressor pulley.
    • If the clutch air gap is not within specification, proceed with adjustment.
  1. Remove the A/C clutch disc and hub bolt.
    1. Hold the A/C clutch disc and hub with the special tool.
    1. Remove A/C clutch disc and hub bolt.
  1. Remove the A/C clutch disc and hub.
    • Thread an 8 x 1.25 mm bolt into the A/C clutch disc and hub to force it from the A/C compressor shaft.
    • Lift the A/C clutch disc and hub and the A/C clutch disc and hub spacer from the A/C compressor shaft.
  1. Add or remove A/C clutch disc and hub spacers based on the measurements taken.
  1. Install the A/C clutch disc and hub.
  1. Install the A/C clutch disc and hub bolt.
    • Hold the A/C clutch disc and hub with the special tool.
    • Install the A/C clutch disc and hub bolt.
  1. Measure the A/C clutch air gap at 3 equally spaced positions between the A/C clutch disc and the A/C compressor pulley.
    • Repeat adjustment procedure if needed.
