Item Part Number Description
1 W500224 Oil level indicator and tube bolt

3 6K873 Oil level indicator and tube
4 — Oil level indicator tube O-ring seal

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the front oil level indicator and tube bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the rear oil level indicator and tube bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Remove the oil level indicator and tube from the cylinder block.
    • Discard the O-ring seal.

  1. NOTE: Lubricate the new oil level indicator tube O-ring seal with clean engine oil prior to installation.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
