SECTION 501-05: Interior Trim and Ornamentation
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 10/29/2005

A-Pillar Trim Panel

Removal and Installation

  1. NOTE: The bolt covers are one time use only.

    Remove the assist handle bolt covers.
    • To remove, start at the handle side of cover and work outward.
  1. Remove the assist handle bolts
    • To install, tighten to 7 Nm (62 lb-in).
  1. Remove the A-pillar trim panel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
