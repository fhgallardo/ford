Procedure revision date: 08/22/2005

Universal Transmitter

The HomeLink® universal transmitter is an integral part of the LH sun visor assembly. This universal transmitter provides a convenient way to substitute up to 3 hand-held transmitters with a single built-in device. The transmitter can learn the radio frequency codes of most current transmitters to operate garage doors, entry gates, security systems, and home or office lighting.
