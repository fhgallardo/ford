SECTION 501-09: Rear View Mirrors
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION
Procedure revision date: 01/10/2005

Rear View Mirrors

The rear view mirror system consists of the following components:

  • Interior rear view mirror
  • Auto-dimming interior rear view mirror with compass (optional)
  • Exterior paddle-style mirror with manually adjusted glass
  • Exterior aero-style mirror with power adjusted glass (optional)
  • Exterior aero-style mirror with power adjusted glass and power folding mirror heads (optional)
  • Exterior mirror plastic housing
  • Exterior mirror glass
  • Exterior mirror control switch (optional — aero-style mirrors only)
  • Heated exterior mirror glass (optional — aero-style mirrors only)
  • Exterior mirror glass adjustment motor (optional — aero-style mirrors only)
  • Exterior mirror head power fold motor (optional and not replaceable — aero-style power fold mirrors only)
