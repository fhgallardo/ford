Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the front driveshaft shield nuts and the front driveshaft shield.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Index-mark the front driveshaft flange to the pinion flange to maintain alignment during installation.
  1. Index-mark the rear driveshaft flange to the transfer case flange to maintain alignment during installation.
  1. Remove the 4 driveshaft flange bolts from the transfer case
    • To install, tighten to 103 Nm (76 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 4 driveshaft flange bolts from the pinion flange.
    • To install, tighten to 103 Nm (76 lb-ft).
  1. Remove and discard the 4 nuts from the front stabilizer bar brackets and lower the rear of the bar.
    • To install, tighten to 48 Nm (35 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the front driveshaft.

  1.  CAUTION: If new bolts are not available, coat the threads of the original bolts with Threadlock and Sealer.

    NOTE: Align the index marks made during removal.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Install new driveshaft flange bolts.
