SECTION 417-04: Daytime Running Lamps
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Daytime Running Lamps (DRL) Resistor


Item Part Number Description
1 W706350 Pin-type retainer
2 8A261 Radiator side air deflector
3 12A581 DRL resistor electrical connector
4 — DRL resistor bolt (part of 14A601)
5 14A601 DRL resistor

Removal and Installation

NOTE: Make sure the headlamp switch and the ignition switch are in the OFF position.

  1. Remove the radiator side air deflector.
    • Remove the pin-type retainer.
  1. Disconnect the DRL resistor electrical connector.
  1. Remove the bolt and the DRL resistor.
    • To install, tighten to 5 Nm (44 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
