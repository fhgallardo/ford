SECTION 303-08: Engine Emission Control
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) System Module Tube — 4.6L (2V)

Removal and Installation

  1. Position the brake booster vacuum hose and bracket aside.
    1. Remove the nut.
      • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
    1. Disconnect the brake booster vacuum hose.
    1. Position the brake booster vacuum hose and bracket aside.
  1. Detach the exhaust manifold-to-exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) system module tube from the exhaust manifold and the EGR system module and remove the tube.
    • To install, tighten to 40 Nm (30 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
