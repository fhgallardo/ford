Removal and Installation

 WARNING: Use of any other than approved DOT 3 motor vehicle brake fluid will cause permanent damage to brake components and will render the brakes inoperative. Failure to follow these instructions may result in personal injury.

 WARNING: Carefully read cautionary information on product label. For EMERGENCY MEDICAL INFORMATION seek medical advice. In the USA or Canada on Ford/Motorcraft products call: 1-800-959-3673. For additional information, consult the product Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) if available. Failure to follow these instructions may result in personal injury.

 CAUTION: Brake fluid is harmful to painted and plastic surfaces. If brake fluid is spilled onto a painted or plastic surface, immediately wash it with water.

 CAUTION: Electronic modules are sensitive to electrical charges. The anti-lock brake system (ABS) module can be damaged if exposed to these charges.

 CAUTION: When installing a new ABS module or HCU, do not use the pump motor harness as a carrying handle. Failure to follow these instructions may result in damage to the HCU assembly.

 CAUTION: When disconnecting the brake lines, do not allow brake fluid to drip onto the ABS module or premature failure may occur.

 CAUTION: Do not allow brake fluid to drip into the ABS module electrical connector cavity or premature failure may occur.

  1. Disconnect the battery ground cable. For additional information, refer to Section 414-01 .
  1. Remove the air cleaner assembly. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .

  1. NOTE: The brake lines must be installed in the same location as removed.

    Remove the 4 brake line-to-HCU fittings.
    • To install, tighten to 17 Nm (13 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the 2 HCU-to-bracket nuts and the HCU.
    • To install, tighten to 9 Nm (80 lb-in).
  1. Remove the ABS module. For additional information, refer to Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) Module in this section.

  1. NOTE: The HCU bracket does not have to be removed in order to complete this procedure.

    If necessary, remove the 3 HCU-bracket-to-frame bolts and the bracket.
    • To install, tighten to 30 Nm (22 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Bleed the brake system. For additional information, refer to Section 206-00 .
