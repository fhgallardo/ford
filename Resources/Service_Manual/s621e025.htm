2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Tailgate Latch Remote Control

Removal and Installation

  1. Open and support the tailgate.
  1. Remove the 8 screws and the tailgate access panel.
    • To install, tighten to 5 Nm (44 lb-in).
  1. Open the 2 clips and release the 2 tailgate latch actuating rods from the tailgate remote control.
  1. Release the 2 tailgate latch actuating rod locators from the tailgate.
  1. Remove the 2 nuts and the tailgate remote control.
    • To install, tighten to 7 Nm (62 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
