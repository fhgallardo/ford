SECTION 303-05: Accessory Drive
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Accessory Drive

The accessory drive:

  • has a single serpentine drive belt with 6 ribs.
  • has automatic tensioning.
  • is not adjustable.
