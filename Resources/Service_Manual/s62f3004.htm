SECTION 415-03: Speakers
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/19/2006

Speaker Enclosure

Regular Cab

Item Part Number Description
1 14A005 Subwoofer enclosure electrical connector
2 4273 Subwoofer enclosure bolt caps (4 required)
3 W709027 Subwoofer enclosure bolts (4 required)
4 18C804 Subwoofer enclosure


SuperCab and Super Crew

Item Part Number Description
1 W709027 Subwoofer enclosure bolts (3 required)
2 18C804 Subwoofer enclosure
3 14A005 Subwoofer enclosure electrical connector

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the 3 bolts (Regular Cab) or 4 bolts (SuperCab and Super Crew) and the subwoofer enclosure.
    • If equipped, remove the 4 bolt caps.
    • Disconnect the electrical connector.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
