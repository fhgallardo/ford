SECTION 303-03: Engine Cooling
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Thermostat — 4.2L


Item Part Number Description
1 15161 Upper radiator hose clamp
2 8260 Upper radiator hose
3 W701607 Coolant outlet connection bolts (2 required)
4 8594 Thermostat housing
5 8255 Thermostat gasket
6 8575 Thermostat

Removal

  1. Drain the cooling system. For additional information, refer to Cooling System Draining, Filling and Bleeding in this section.
  1. Release the clamp and disconnect the upper radiator hose from the thermostat housing.
  1. Remove the bolts and the thermostat housing.
  1. Remove the gasket and the thermostat.

Installation

  1.  CAUTION: Do not use metal scrapers, wire brushes, power abrasive discs, or other abrasive means to clean the sealing surfaces. These may cause scratches and gouges resulting in leak paths. Use a plastic scraper to clean the sealing surfaces.

    Clean the gasket mating surfaces with a plastic scraper and metal surface cleaner. Follow the directions on the packaging.

  1. NOTE: The thermostat is indexed and must be installed as shown.

    Install the thermostat in the thermostat housing and rotate the thermostat either clockwise or counterclockwise to engage the cam on the thermostat securely. The thermostat vent must be located either at the 11 o'clock or 1 o'clock position.
    1. Thermostat vent shown at 11 o'clock.
    1. Thermostat vent shown at 1 o'clock.
  1. Position a new gasket and the thermostat housing and install the bolts. Tighten the bolts in 2 stages.
    • Stage 1: Tighten to 8 Nm (71 lb-in).
    • Stage 2: Tighten an additional 60 degrees.
  1. Connect the upper radiator hose and position the clamp.
  1. Fill and bleed the cooling system. For additional information, refer to Cooling System Draining, Filling and Bleeding in this section.
