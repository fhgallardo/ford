SECTION 206-03: Front Disc Brake
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/07/2006

Brake Disc — Rear Wheel Drive (RWD)

Removal

  1.  CAUTION: Do not allow the caliper and anchor plate assembly to hang from the brake hose or damage to the hose can occur.

    Remove the 2 brake caliper anchor plate bolts and position the caliper and anchor plate assembly aside.
    • Support the caliper and anchor plate assembly using mechanic's wire.
  1. Remove and discard the cotter pin from the spindle nut retainer.
  1. Remove the spindle nut retainer and the nut.
    • Discard the spindle nut.
  1. Remove the front brake disc from the spindle.

Installation

  1. Position the brake disc on the spindle.

  1. NOTE: Tighten the spindle nut to the specified torque. Rotate the disc counterclockwise 5 rotations and then recheck the torque.

    Install a new spindle nut.
    • Tighten to 400 Nm (295 lb-ft).
  1. Install the spindle nut retainer and a new cotter pin.
  1. Position the brake caliper and anchor plate assembly and install 2 new bolts.
    • Tighten to 200 Nm (148 lb-ft).
