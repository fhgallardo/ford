Removal and Installation

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the LH exhaust manifold. For additional information, refer to Valve Cover — LH in this section.
  1. Remove the oil level indicator tube bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Remove the oil level indicator tube and discard the O-ring seal.

  1. NOTE: Install a new O-ring seal. Lubricate the O-ring seal with clean engine oil prior to installation.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
