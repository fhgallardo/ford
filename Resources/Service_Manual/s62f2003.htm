SECTION 415-02: Antenna
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Antenna Lead-In Cable


Item Part Number Description
1 13A506 Pin-type retainers
2 18812 Antenna lead-in cable

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the audio unit. For additional information, refer to Section 415-01 .
  1. Lower the glove compartment.
    • Press the side tabs and release the glove compartment.
  1. Disconnect the antenna lead-in cable from the antenna cable.
  1. Disconnect the pin-type retainers and remove the cable.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
