SECTION 206-03: Front Disc Brake
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Brake Disc Shield

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the brake disc. For additional information, refer to Brake Disc — Four Wheel Drive (4WD) or Brake Disc — Rear Wheel Drive (RWD) in this section.
  1. Remove the 3 brake disc shield bolts and the brake disc shield.
    • To install, tighten to 12 Nm (9 lb-ft).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
