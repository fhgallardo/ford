2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Brake Pedal Motor Cable

Removal and Installation

  1. NOTE: The accelerator pedal and the brake pedal must be in the same position before connecting the adjustable brake pedal cable.

    Release the brake pedal motor cable from the brake pedal transmission assembly.
  1. Release the brake pedal motor cable from the brake pedal motor.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
