SECTION 501-03: Body Closures
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
GENERAL PROCEDURES
Procedure revision date: 02/02/2005

Door Alignment — Front

All adjustments

  1. Remove the door latch striker.

Horizontal and vertical adjustment

  1. Loosen the front door hinge-to-front door bolts.
  1. Adjust the front door.
  1. Tighten the front door hinge-to-front door bolts to 30 Nm (22 lb-ft).

Fore and aft adjustment

  1. Remove the front fender. For additional information, refer to Section 501-02 .
  1. Loosen the door hinge-to-body bolts.
  1. Adjust the front door.
  1. Tighten the front door hinge-to-body bolts to 30 Nm (22 lb-ft).
  1. Install the front fender. For additional information, refer to Section 501-02 .

All adjustments

  1. Install the front door latch striker.
    • Tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
