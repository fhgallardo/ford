Disassembly

  1. Remove the halfshaft. For additional information, refer to Halfshaft in this section.
  1. Remove the boot clamps.
  1. Separate the boot from the inboard constant velocity (CV) joint housing.
  1. Remove the retaining ring.
  1. Remove the inboard CV joint housing.
  1. Index-mark the inner race and ball cage.
  1. Remove the 6 balls.
  1. Remove the snap ring.
  1. Remove the inner race and the ball cage.
  1. Remove the inboard boot.
  1. Remove the boot clamps.
  1. Remove the outboard boot.

  1. NOTE: If the grease is contaminated, clean and inspect the joint for wear. Install a new outboard CV joint and interconnecting shaft assembly if worn/damaged.

    Check the grease for contamination.

Assembly

  1. Position the outboard boot on the shaft.
  1. Pack the outboard CV joint with 165 grams (5.82 ounces) of grease.
    1. Pack the outboard CV joint with grease.
    1. Spread any remaining grease from the kit evenly inside the boot.
  1. Install the outboard boot.
    1. Clean the boot mounting surface.
    1. Seat the boot in the CV joint boot groove.
  1. Position the boot clamps.

  1. NOTE: Tighten the through-bolt until the tool is in the closed position.

    Using the special tool, install the 2 boot clamps.
  1. Position the boot clamp on the shaft.
  1. Position the inboard boot on the shaft.
  1. Position the ball cage on the shaft with the tapered end facing the boot.

  1. NOTE: Align the index marks.

    Position the inner race on the shaft with the counterbored end facing the boot.
  1. Install the snap ring.
  1. Lubricate and position the 6 balls with grease.
  1. Fill the inboard CV joint housing with 235 grams (8.29 ounces) of grease.
  1. Position the boot clamp on the inboard CV joint housing.
  1. Position the inboard CV joint housing on the ball and race assembly.
  1. Install the retaining ring.
  1. Remove any excess grease from the mating surface. Seat the boot in the CV joint boot groove.
  1. Set the halfshaft assembled length to specification. For additional information, refer to Specifications in this section.

  1.  CAUTION: Do not damage the boot.

    Insert a dull screwdriver blade under the boot to release the pressure.

  1. NOTE: Tighten the through-bolt until the tool is in the closed position.

    Using the special tool, install the 2 boot clamps.
  1. Install the halfshaft. For additional information, refer to Halfshaft in this section.
