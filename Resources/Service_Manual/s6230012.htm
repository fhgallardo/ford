2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
GENERAL PROCEDURES
Procedure revision date: 12/07/2005

Camshaft Surface Inspection

  1. Inspect camshaft lobes for pitting or damage in the contact area. Minor pitting is acceptable outside the contact area.
    • If excessive pitting or damage is present, install new components as necessary. Refer to the appropriate section in Group 303 for the procedure.
