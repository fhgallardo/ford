Removal and Installation

All vehicles

  1. Disconnect the license plate lamp electrical connectors.
  1. If equipped, disconnect the trailer harness electrical connectors.
  1. If equipped, detach the trailer harness pin-type retainer from the rear bumper.
  1. If equipped, disconnect the rear parking aid electrical connector.

Early build Mark LT

  1. NOTE: Anytime the rear bumper arm-to-rear bumper bolts are removed, discard and install new rear bumper arm-to-rear bumper bolts.

    If equipped, remove the rear bumper arm-to-rear bumper bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 175 Nm (129 lb-ft).

Late build Mark LT and all F-150

  1. NOTE: Anytime the rear bumper nuts are removed, discard and install new rear bumper nuts.

    Remove the rear bumper nuts.
    • To install, tighten to 175 Nm (129 lb-ft).

All vehicles

  1. Remove the rear bumper.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Transfer components as necessary. If equipped with a rear parking aid sensor, refer to Section 413-13 for additional information.
