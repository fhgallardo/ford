SECTION 308-07A: Four Wheel Drive (4WD) Systems
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Mode Select Switch (MSS)

  1. Remove the mode select switch (MSS) from the instrument panel.
    • Pry the switch out of the instrument panel.
    • Disconnect the harness connector.
    • Remove the MSS switch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
