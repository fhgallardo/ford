Both sides

  1. With the vehicle in NEUTRAL, position it on a hoist. For additional information, refer to Section 100-02 .
  1. Remove the air cleaner outlet pipe. For additional information, refer to Section 303-12 .
  1. Remove the radiator site shield.
  1. Remove the bolts and position the fan shroud on the fan.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Remove the LH engine support insulator through bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 350 Nm (258 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: Make sure the RH engine support insulator mating surfaces and the washer mating surface are free of foreign material and corrosion before installation. Install a new washer.

    Remove the RH engine support insulator nuts and washer.
    • To install, tighten to 250 Nm (184 lb-ft).
  1. Remove the A/C compressor and power steering pump bracket brace upper nut and install the special tool.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).

  1. NOTE: Use a 10 mm (0.39 in) spring link to connect the Engine Lifting Bracket to the 3-Bar Engine Support.

    Install the special tool and lift the engine approximately 75 mm (3 in).

RH side

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator-to-bracket mating surfaces and the bolt holes in the bracket of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Remove the bolts and the RH engine support insulator.
    • Apply Threadlock 262 to the bolts threads prior to installation.
    • To install, tighten to 115 Nm (85 lb-ft).

LH side

  1.  CAUTION: Clean the engine support insulator-to-bracket mating surfaces and the bolt holes in the bracket of any dirt or foreign material prior to installation.

    Remove the bolts and the LH engine support insulator.
    • Apply Threadlock 262 to the bolts threads prior to installation.
    • To install, tighten to 115 Nm (85 lb-ft).

Both sides

  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
