SECTION 501-04: Pickup Bed and Platform Body
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 10/10/2006

Pickup Bed

NOTE: Eight-foot pickup bed shown, all others similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 W701082-S900 Ground bolt
2 180103-14405 Electrical connector
3 170308-13550 License plate lamp connectors
4 13A576 Trailer harness connector
5 — Fuel fill tube clamp
6 W709654-S424 Screw (3 required)
7 9030 Fuel door assembly
8 W709424-S900 Bolt (8 required)
9 — Pickup box
10 W708606-S436 RH/ W708605-S436 LH Attachment clip (8 required)
  1. Remove the ground bolt.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Disconnect the main tail lamp connector and reverse parking aid, if equipped.
  1. Disconnect the license lamp connectors.
  1. If equipped, disconnect the trailer harness connector(s) from the rear bumper.
  1. Remove the fuel filler tube-to-pickup bed clamp.
    • To install, tighten to 7 Nm (62 lb-in).
  1. Remove the 3 fuel filler neck screws.

  1.  CAUTION: Remove the fuel fill door assembly. Otherwise, damage to the fuel fill tube and fuel door assembly can occur when the pickup bed is removed.

    Remove the fuel door assembly.
    • Press in the 2 retaining tabs on top of the fuel door assembly.
    • Slide the fuel door assembly out of the pickup bed.

  1. NOTE: The pickup bed bolts are one time use only. Install new bolts whenever the bolts are removed.

    NOTE: All 8-foot pickup beds have 8 bolts; all others have 6 bolts.

    Remove and discard the 8 pickup box bolts.
    • To install, tighten to 80 Nm (59 lb-ft).

  1.  CAUTION: To avoid damaging the fuel fill tube, guide the fuel fill tube out of the fuel door opening as the pickup bed is removed.

    Remove the pickup box.
    • Guide the fuel fill tube out of the fuel door opening.

  1. NOTE: New attachment clips must be installed whenever the pickup box is removed. All 8-foot beds will have 8 attachment clips. All other beds will have 6 attachment clips.

    Install new attachment clips before reinstalling the pickup box.

  1. NOTE: Install a new fuel filler tube clamp if specified torque is not obtained or if part strips during reinstallation.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
