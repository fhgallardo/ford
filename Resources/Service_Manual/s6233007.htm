Removal and Installation

  1. Drain the engine cooling system. For additional information, refer to Cooling System Draining, Filling and Bleeding in this section.
  1. Remove the intake manifold. For additional information, refer to Section 303-01C .
  1. Remove the bolts and the bypass tube.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Remove and inspect the gaskets. Install new gaskets if necessary.

  1. NOTE: Clean the gasket mating surfaces with metal surface prep. Follow the directions on the packaging.

    To install, reverse the removal procedure.
