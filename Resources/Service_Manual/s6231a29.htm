SECTION 303-01A: Engine — 4.2L
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
IN-VEHICLE REPAIR
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Flexplate

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the transmission. For additional information, refer to Section 307-01 .
  1. Remove the bolts and the flexplate.
    • To install, tighten to 80 Nm (59 lb-ft) in a criss-cross pattern.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
