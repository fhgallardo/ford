SECTION 501-05: Interior Trim and Ornamentation
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 01/20/2006

Cowl Side Trim Panel

Removal and Installation

Both sides

  1. Remove the front door scuff plate.

LH side

  1. Remove the cover plate.

RH side

  1. Remove the fuse panel access door.
  1. Remove the pin-type retainer from the cowl trim panel.

Both sides

  1. Remove the cowl trim panel.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
