SECTION 310-01: Fuel Tank and Lines
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Inertia Fuel Shutoff (IFS) Switch


Item Part Number Description
1 — Inertia fuel shutoff (IFS) switch electrical connector
2 — IFS bolts (2 required)
3 9341 IFS switch

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the RH lower A-pillar trim panel.
  1. Disconnect the inertia fuel shutoff (IFS) switch electrical connector.
  1. Remove the 2 IFS bolts and the IFS switch.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
