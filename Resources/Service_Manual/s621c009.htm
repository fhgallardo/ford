SECTION 501-12: Instrument Panel and Console
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Console — Overhead

NOTE: Modular overhead console shown, all other consoles similar.


Item Part Number Description
1 18519D42 Sliding storage bin
2 18519A70 Overhead console
3 14358 Overhead console electrical connectors
4 13N701 Dome lamp assembly
5 14358 Dome lamp electrical connector

Removal and Installation

NOTE: If removing the track assembly, remove the headliner. For additional information, refer to Section 501-05 .

  1. Depress the side tabs and remove the overhead storage bin.
  1. Remove the overhead console.
  1. Disconnect the 2 overhead console electrical connectors.
  1. Remove the dome lamp assembly.
  1. Disconnect the dome lamp electrical connector.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
