Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the ignition coil. For additional information, refer to Section 303-07A .
  1. Disconnect the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve hose and electrical connector.
    • Detach the wiring harness retainer from the valve cover stud bolt.
  1. Remove the nuts and the engine lifting eye.
    • To install, tighten to 25 Nm (18 lb-ft).
  1. Detach the spark plug wire retainers.
  1. Remove the bolt and position the radio interference capacitor aside.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Remove the bolts, the stud bolts and the RH valve cover.
    • To install, tighten to 10 Nm (89 lb-in).
  1. Remove and discard the RH valve cover gasket.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
    • Install a new RH valve cover gasket.
