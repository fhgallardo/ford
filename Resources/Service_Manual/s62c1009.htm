SECTION 412-01: Climate Control
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Plenum Chamber


Item Part Number Description
1 — Mode door actuator electrical connectors (2 required)
2 W701696-S437 Upper plenum chamber screws (3 required)
3 W701696-S437 Lower plenum chamber screws (5 required)
4 18471 Plenum chamber

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the heater core and evaporator core housing. For additional information, refer to Heater Core And Evaporator Core Housing in this section.
  1. Disconnect the 2 mode door actuator electrical connectors, detach the routing clip and position the wire harness aside.
  1. Remove the 3 upper plenum chamber screws.
  1. Remove the 5 lower plenum chamber screws and the plenum.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
