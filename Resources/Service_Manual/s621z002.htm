SECTION 501-35: Body Repairs
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION
Procedure revision date: 08/12/2005

Body

The body consists of the following:

  • Cab-forward design
  • Aluminum hood
  • Magnesium radiator support
  • Lightweight steel welded panel construction
  • Bolted, removable front fenders, hinged doors, tailgate and hood
  • Full frame bolted to the body
  • Regular Cab, SuperCab and SuperCrew, cab styles
  • SuperCrew has a standard 4-door cab
  • SuperCrew has a standard 1.67 m (5.5 ft) pickup bed length
  • 3,198 mm (125.9 in) wheelbase is available with 1.98 m (6.5 ft) Styleside or Flareside pickup bed length
  • 3,365 mm (132.48 in) wheelbase is available with 1.67 m (5.5 ft) Styleside pickup bed length
  • 3,518 mm (138.5 in) wheelbase is available with 1.67 m (5.5 ft) Styleside pickup bed length
  • 3,671 mm (144.52 in) wheelbase is available with 1.98 m (6.5 ft) Styleside and Flareside pickup bed length. The 3,671 mm (144.52 in) wheelbase also has a 2.44 m (8.0 ft) Styleside pickup bed length available
  • 3,810 mm (150 in) wheelbase is available with 1.98 m (6.5 ft) Styleside pickup bed length with SuperCrew cab
  • 4,143 mm (163.11 in) wheelbase is available with 2.44 m (8.0 ft) Styleside pickup bed length
  • Lincoln Mark LT available with 3,518 mm (138.5 in) wheelbase and 1.67 m (5.5 ft) Styleside pickup bed
  • Lincoln Mark LT available in SuperCrew cab style only
  • Lincoln Mark LT has unique chrome bodyside cladding
  • Lincoln Mark LT has unique tailgate reflectors
  • Lincoln Mark LT has unique chrome pickup box rail bars
