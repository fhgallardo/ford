SECTION 413-08: Information and Message Center
2006 F-150/Mark LT Workshop Manual
GENERAL PROCEDURES
Procedure revision date: 02/18/2005

Compass Zone Adjustment

 

With Message Center

  1. NOTE: The compass zone setting is preset from the factory to Zone 8.

    Determine which magnetic zone you are in for your geographic location.
  1. Locate the compass module at the base of the mirror.
  1. Turn the ignition key to the ON position.

  1. NOTE: To exit the compass zone adjustment before carrying out the compass zone adjustment procedure, press the INFO button on the message center switch.

    Press and hold the reset button on the LH top of the compass module until RESET FOR ZONE and the current setting (1-15) appears in the message center display.
  1. Press the SETUP button on the message center switch repeatedly until the correct geographic location for your area is displayed in the message center.
  1. Press the RESET button to exit the zone setting procedure and to lock your selected zone setting. The message center displays CALIBRATION COMPLETE.
 

Without Message Center

  1. NOTE: The compass zone setting is preset from the factory to Zone 8.

    Determine which magnetic zone you are in for your geographic location.
  1. Locate the compass module at the base of the mirror.
  1. Turn the ignition key to the ON position.
  1. Press and hold the reset button on the LH top of the compass module until the current setting (1-15) area appears in the instrument cluster integrated display.
  1. Press the reset button repeatedly to cycle through the zone settings until the correct setting for your geographic location is displayed.
  1. Release pressure on the reset button to exit the zone setting mode. After approximately 5 seconds the display reports the direction and the zone is set.
