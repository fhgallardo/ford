REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Door Ajar Switch


Item Part Number Description
1 14018/10B997 Door ajar switch (RH/LH)

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the front or rear door latch. For additional information, refer to Front Door Latch or Rear Door Latch — SuperCrew in this section.
  1. Release the locking tab and remove the door ajar switch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
