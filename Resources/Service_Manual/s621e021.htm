REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
Procedure revision date: 06/29/2005

Rear Door Lock Actuator

Removal and Installation

  1. Remove the rear door latch. For additional information, refer to Rear Door Latch — SuperCrew in this section.
  1. Remove the rear door lock actuator from the rear door latch.
  1. To install, reverse the removal procedure.
